Believers

Overview

The Believers guard the moving stones, and make sure no one disturbs the Delvers, who are entombed down here. The Believers watch the moving stones carefully, because it’s a bad sign when they move, and they have to figure out what it means to advert danger.

 

They were a secret group that was lead by Larrakh. Dornen and Ilmeth did kill the people found in the cave. Grund was caught, but he was not actually the one who killed them. Grund was manipulated by the Believers that he was helping by trying to hide the bodies.

 

  • Elak Dornen: The de facto leader. He is stern and inflexible.Sent to Waterdeep to serve trial.
  • Ilmeth Waelvur: Hard-drinking and sullen. Sent to Waterdeep to serve trial.
  • Ulhro Luruth: Runs a Tannery in Red Larch. He was interrigated by the Heroes to give up the Believers.
  • Grund: This goofy half-orc is a sort of hired goon for The Believers.
  • Baragustas: He was one of the original Believers. He oversaw that the rituals the group performed.
  • Rotharr: He is known for the day of the collapse. His son, Braelen, was going through the rituals of becoming a Believer. Tyelknowle caught him and his son sneaking out of the tunnels.
