Mirabar Delegation

Overview

The delegation traveled overland from Westbridge to Beliard, crossing the Stone Bridge. From Beliard it headed south into the Sumber Hills, bound for Summit Hall. They were heading to Red Larch next, but their trip got cut short.

The Delegation was attacked first by the Earth Cults. But at the same time they were also attacked by the Air Cults.

 

The attacks didn’t stop there. Later other cults kidnapped or traded to move other members of the delegation around.

The delegation was roughly around 35-40 people. Not all of them were Dwarvin.
