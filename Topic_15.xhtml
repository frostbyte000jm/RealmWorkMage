Game 07

Game Day

Detailed Entry

The next morning everyone woke up to travel toward Beliard, when the noticed something wasn't right with Rowena . Rowena started speaking in a Canadian/Irish/Japanize/whoKnows accent, which is what a Dwarf stuck in an Elf's body would sound like (Trust me, this is true).

 

The Crew started questioning Rowena what is going on. Ty was convinced she could have been possessed. Barikhol got a grand idea to get her drunk and let her pass out. Then they can properly decide what to do with her. This plan backfired, when Barikhol didn't factor in drinking so early in the morning and so quickly. He was the dwarf that passed out instead.

 

They decided to take Rowena to Kaylessa . Kaylessa knew a lot of the arcane so the heroes hoped she would know what to do. Kaylessa had no idea, but she knew Brother Eardon may. She sent a servant up to his room. Brother Eardon studied in the Necromantic arts for his entire life.

 

Once he came down he started talking to the heroes and looked over at Rowena . After seeing into her eyes he told the heroes to hold a moment. Brother Eardon did a gesture with his hand and hit Rowena on the forehead. "Miss do you know a second soul inhabits your body?"

 

Rowena really confused how she got here started asking "Where am…"

 

"Miss, this is important" Brother Eardon pressed on, "Do you know more than one soul shares your body?"

 

Rowena looks at him, "Yes."

 

Others started asking her questions and at some point Rowena switched back in to Odert . Odert was the Dwarf Bard that used to travel in Rowena 's party until they were brutally murdered by a necromancer. The theory is that the necromancer used a ritual to make two souls share the same body. The party doesn't know why, and the necromancer is dead so they can't ask him.

 

The Heroes decided to travel and figure out how to help Rowena and Odert later.

 

Stannor let the heroes borrow a horse and a cart. They headed north up the long road.

 

About 4 hours into their journey Ty noticed something wasn't right. Little things like reflection off the hills that shouldn't be there. Quick movement of something off in the distance. He realized this is the start of an ambush. Ty warned the others.

 

Just then a tree fell in front of the wagon, Josephus knew he could make it around so he got the horse going. This took some maneuvering and the heroes noticed the other horses coming up the rear. Once the heroes got going the highwaymen started gaining on them.

 

They started casting an shooting. Odert wasn't quite used to the sorcerer casting and blasted off a spell. The cart got shrouded in fog. Odert was trying to start a rock slide, she casted her earth hand and it pulled away some rocks. Odert didn't get to see the effects till later.

 

Once the fog dissipated the noticed there were not as many highwaymen.

 

After some more fighting, one of the highwaymen noticed Ty. He was a little curious how Ty got there, but these two have a bad past. Expo jumped to attack Ty.

 

During the fight Odert casted another spell and her (their?) wild magic went off again. This time it went out of control changing every few seconds.

 

A head Josephus saw a man with a cart, and he had to make a decision. Miss the man, and most likely crash OR go straight and the man will have a chance to jump out of the way, but they will not crash.

 

Josephus did the honorable thing and maneuvered out of the way ( Josephus should gain an inspiration). They crashed and the highwaymen caught up. The heroes began fighting the highwaymen. Rowena noticed that she had a very dangerous spell about to cast and her knowledge in the arcane told her she could run far enough to protect two of her friends.

 

She ran and was able to pull the blast radius away from Armond and Tyelknowle . The blast pretty much took care of all the highwaymen, but left a wound on Josephus and Barikhol .

 

Ty picked up Expo and brought him over in front of the rest of the heroes. He told them about Expos evil doings and asked if any there do not believe Expo should not be put down. None of the adventures stood up for Expo and Ty Executed him.

 

Barikhol gathered up some horses to ride to Beliard and they went inside the Larch Path Inn to cleanup and rest.

 

The next adventure will begin as the heroes arrive in Beliard.
