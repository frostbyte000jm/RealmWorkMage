Game 05

Game Day

Game Date: 11/23/15

Date in Play: (5) Mirtul 02, 1491

TLDR

  • A sinkhole opened up in the middle of town.
  • Children were trapped down there.
  • After saving them they explored the caves.
  • There were rats, dead people, floating rocks, goblins and an Earth Mage.
  • Larrakh kidnapped Barikhol .
  • The heroes lost Larrakh but saved their friend.
  • The Heroes questioned Ulhro about who the Believers are.

Detailed Entry

The heroes start at the breakfast table. Armond , Josephus , and Tyelknowle . Rowena was still in bed and Barikhol … well no one was sure where Barikhol was. Armond was talking about a certain person he meet. His name is Brother Eardon . Brother Eardon talked about some friends he knew that were part of a delegation from Mirabar and they were in Beliard about two tendays ago. They were planning on leaving in a couple days and head toward Red Larch . The Delegation should have been here by now.

 

While the group was hashing out the details, there was a loud boom. A sinkhole managed to form in the middle of the road. There was a wagon and children on it. It fell into the hole. One child had cuts, scrapes and maybe a couple bruises. The other Child was unconscious.

 

The heroes ran up to the hole but were stopped by some town locals. They were saying things like "Everything is fine, we will help get this children out. They will be ok down there for a little bit. No need to worry." when the heroes tried to push pass them they put up a little resistance. They were no match for the heroes and they walked past them like it was no problem. One of them said "I am going to go get help." another said "The Delvers cannot be disturbed."

 

Josephus stepped up to the other two and one of them started to mouth off to Josephus . Josephus told him to shut up, pointed his finger in his face, and demanded he back off. That man also decided to go get help. The last one was about to leave when Josephus grabbed him and told him, "You are going to tell us what is going on here and you're going to tell me quick."

 

Josephus was very intimidating. This man almost wet his pants. "The delvers are down there. They are prophets. They tell us when bad things are going to happen. If you disturb them they will get really mad." Josephus told him to “stay right here until I get back.”

 

While this exchange was happening, Tyelknowle went down there with the help of Armond . After getting down there he lifted up the wagon and helped pull the kids out. Ty cast a healing spell on the girl and it woke her up. Meanwhile Rowena made her way down to the hole with her gear. She started putting together a bag to help pull the children up. A local offered his wood carrying bag. Using her rope she made a nice little basket and sent it down. Armond assisted Rowena in pulling up the two children and sent them off with Lady Auldarhk , the follower of Sune. She took the two children and their families back to the church.

 

Ty asked Rowena to also run to the church and grab his gear. Rowena skipped off to the church to find his backpack and things lying next to his bed. She carried them on back to the site and lowered them down to him. Ty got changed into his armor and gear.

 

The Heroes descended down into the hole. They saw a big stone door and a path that leads off to the North. They decided to see where the Norther path leads to first. After they get to the top it ends with a small wooden coffer (empty) and a heap of sand with torches put out in it. Peeking out the door they are in the yard of Waelvur's Wagonworks . They talked about it for a moment and decided that they should adventure further into the tunnels.

 

:::Cut Scene:::

The first person that ran off is named Ilmeth . Ilmeth went to get help and shows up in front of a door. He gives it a knock. A person answers it. You can tell the person is male by his voice, but his face isn't shown. Ilmeth moves his hands up in front of his body and a second later the person that answers the door does the same thing.

 

"Why are you here?" the mystery person says to Ilmeth .

 

"I am here because those nosy adventurers had made their way into the cave." Ilmeth replied.

 

"How?" the mystery man says, looking over his shoulders and making a gesture.

 

"There was a cave in." Ilmeth explained.

 

"We are on it. We will show those heroes what curiosity earns them."

:::End Cut Scene:::

 

The heroes approach the big stone door and Rowena tries to pull it open. Earlier Tyelknowle placed a piton at the base of the door they forgot about. It wasn't working. Josephus goes up to the door, tries, and notices the piton. Embarrassed, Josephus pulls the piton and pockets it. Josephus also pulls out his new DriftGlobe and says "Light". It begins to glow.

 

They make their way inside and carefully walk down the hallway. At the end of the hallway there are two stone statues of dwarfs the statues are on a rail system that they can be pushed and it will open up the pathway, but block off the north or south paths.

 

The heroes decided to go North. Up this path they come to a curve. While at the curve Tyelknowle notices a faint smell of rotting flesh. They make their way deeper into the cave and come upon some bodies. There was also Rats. The heroes could hear them in the walls and see two on the bodies.

 

When approaching the room Rowena reaches out with her mage hand and pokes one of the Rats. The Rats go into a frenzy and attack. The Heroes made quick work of this vermin and took minor injuries. They probably should keep an eye on those wounds for the next couple of days, rats can have awful diseases.

 

Armond takes one quick look at the bodies (human) and flips the over to see a carving of a triangle with a notch on the bottom that points up. This is the marking of the Cult of the Black Earth .

 

Armond fills with rage and curses. He stands up and quickly makes it to the next room. In the center of the room was a floating rock. The rock was heavy and would probably take multiple creatures to lift it, but for some reason it was floating in the center of the room.

 

Armond wanted nothing to do with it and continued to press on. Rowena on the other hand wanted to investigate it. She tried manipulating it with her mage hand, but couldn't do much other than nudge it ever so slightly. Tyelknowle went halfway down the hall so he could keep an eye on Rowena and Armond at the same time.

 

Ty was trying to calm Armond explaining that "you should not allow your emotions to control you. Calm down, we will catch these people." Ty tried asking what is going on.

 

Armond gave answers that the Earth Cult is here and they need to be put down. He wouldn't explain too much, because he wanted to find them. Talking was a waste of time when there are evil cultist here that need to be destroyed.

 

In the next room there was a large statue of a dwarf in chainmail. The statue, not to scale, stood tall with a battle axe in hand. It appears that someone took a huge strike to the center of this statue because there is a chuck taken out of the dwarf’s abdomen.

 

In the abdomen there was a parcel. It was a bag that had a lot of coins and another item. Armond walked up and grabbed the knife and read the name out loud, " Reszur ". The knife begin to light up. Armond stumbled back realizing he could have made a mistake saying the script on this knife. Objects can sometimes have curses on them and when read they can do things to you. He chanced it and said the name again, " Reszur ". The light went out.

 

(Side note: Characters are not infallible. They make mistakes just like we do in real life. I accept actions the players make as actions the characters make. I would not doom your character with something as silly as this. If this was a spell that could have hurt you, cursed you, spelled you, or more, there would have been some kind of roll to save you. I always wonder what kind of GM, DM, or Storytellers, players are used to having that has made them so paranoid. Or are they themselves that evil of a storyteller that they would have killed a character where she/he stands by speaking a name on a knife?)

 

He placed the knife back in the pile saying " Kaylessa can identify this for us."

 

Ty went to go check on one door on the west side of the room. He wasn't sure, but he could swear he heard something in there. Armond went to check the other door and heard nothing, until the door opened up in his face.

 

Acting quickly Armond pushed the door closed yelling "Really?" Everyone got ready and Armond backed up letting them in.

 

The door flew open and combat began. The enemies were earth cultist. They wore brown cloaks with hoods. Three of them had symbols drawn on their cloaks signaling them as sorcerers. They had Mud Magic. They said "We are the Bringers of Woe , we have come to reward your curiosity." They fought and fought hard. These mud sorcerers could bend earth to their will. They could pull rocks from the ground and from around the room. Some could throw poison.

 

Rowena pulled out her new spell she has been practicing with the others all week. She, of course, could not use it to it's full potential during sparing but there was nothing stopping her now. A hand shaped from the earth came busting out of the ground splitting into two garbing one of the Earth Cultist and started crushing him. He screamed in terror and his screams became muffled by the sounds of his crushing bones and blood pouring out of his mouth, ears, nose, and pants.

 

When this happened Armond felt pressure from something that was hot. He didn't know why. But it happened when Rowena would kill something with her new ability.

 

The last cultist decided to run, slamming the east door behind him. He yelled "Master we have a problem."

 

Tyelknowle ran to the door and shoved it open. Armond blasted the fleeing mage and he exploded into dust. The mud sorcerers would turn to dust when they are killed. The other cultist would just shrink to the ground.

 

The room to the east was lit and Josephus was able to put away his Drift Globe. Ty split his image into two and started to approach the only person he saw in the room. Goblins started shooting arrows at him as he passed the rocks they were hiding behind. Some of the arrows went through the image, but some of them hit the real Ty.

 

Ty had his image heal him and Ty told him "Thank you."

His image replied, "You are certainly welcome."

Ty said "You look mighty handsome today."

His image replied, "Why yes I know."

 

This actually went on for a while, but the others were still in combat around them. The Earth Cultist in the room picked up his Glave and said "Who interrupts my studies?" the heroes responded with attacks.

 

At this time a couple heroes could see Barikhol . They were questioning earlier where Barikhol was, but assumed he was doing some of his monk duties, making ale, drinking ale, or taking a nap. They didn't realize he had been captured. Barikhol was chained up (for lack of a better word) with stones that bound his hands, legs, and neck together. He looked injured, but alive.

 

Armond sent an eldritch blast at the Earth Mage and he swiped it out of the way. "Amateurs."

 

Armond said "I didn't even know someone could deflect that blast." The Earth mage stood there with a smug look on his face. He then released a mighty blast of sonic energy from between Rowena and Armond . This ringed in their ears and caused extreme pressure on their bodies.

 

Josephus fought and took out a Goblin. Rowena also grabbed a Goblin with her earth hand and crushed it. The goblin tried to take another shot at Rowena but the hand wrapped around him caused him to miss.

 

The Earth Mage sent his other Goblins to attack the heroes and started making his way to the south east corner of the room. Armond seeing the earth mage back up decided to pull out the wand of magic missiles and aim them with almost all the power he could pull from the wand. Eight bolts of missiles flew out of the wand and streaked towards the earth mage. As a reaction the earth mage pulled all the power he could from the earth to form a shield around him absorbing all eight missiles. Each blast breaking a piece of his magical shield.

 

"Son of a Bitch!" cried Armond .

 

The earth mage not sure of what other tricks the heroes had and not wanting to find out he made his escape. Before leaving he told Barikhol "Your friends came for you. Looks like you have been saved, this time." and the mage disappeared.

 

The heroes freed Barikhol and got him some healing. Looking around the lit room they saw 6 giant men wrapped up in cloth and laid in some kind of a burial ritual around the edges of the room. They must have been there for a while they smelled musty, but not of decay.

 

After a little bit of discussion they decided that taking Barikhol to the surface was the best action. They need to regroup and heal up a little bit.

 

On their way out Ty and Rowena grabbed a couple rats and came out of the cellar doors into Waelvur's Wagonworks . They said, looking at the curious people standing there watching them appear out of a door that most thought was storage, "Did you all know you have a big rat problem? These things are huge. Don't worry we got them. There were 5."

 

Once the heroes made it back to the main street they saw a man sitting there nervously. It was the man that Josephus told to “stay right here until I get back.”

 

They continued to the Tavern and Josephus , Ty, and Rowena agreed to question the man. Ty and Rowena helped get Barikhol off to bed for some rest and they all meet at the tavern after a few moments. Armond decided to go wash up real quick and meet them there.

 

At the tavern there were three adventures, covered in blood, dirty, and geared up talking to this man who is named Ulhro (All-her-o). Josephus is laying on the intimidation think and this man is scared.

 

Josephus told him, "we went down there, we found your delvers, we killed them. They were weak so they are not going to protect you. You can talk to us about what is going on."

 

Ulhro said "You killed them?" Josephus nodding, "but that is impossible."

 

Josephus replied, "oh no man. We did it. They are dead. We turned them to dust."

 

Ulhro replied, "I don't think you understand. The Delvers are dead. They have been dead for many generations." Josephus obviously confused pressed Ulhro to explain.

 

"There is a group in the town called the Belivers," Ulhro started nervously looking around the room as he spoke. "They…"

 

Harburk shows up at the table, "Is everything alright here?" he questions the characters allowing his gaze to drift over Ulhro for a moment's hesitation. Everyone, including Ulhro said they were fine. "I noticed you all jumped down into the hole to rescue those poor kids. But I never saw you come out." Harbark was making a statement, but everyone heard the unanswered question.

 

Paranoid of not knowing who to trust, they decided to lie to Harburk . "You must have missed us. There was a bit of confusion." Harburk not believing them, but allowing them to have their privacy didn't press on. "Thank you for rescuing those children. I have some men out there trying to figure out what to do about this big hole in the street."

 

Ulhro continued "The Believers are a select group who meet on a regular bases to interpret the stones. The Delvers move the stones to give us warning when bad things are coming. People have died when we have not listen to their warnings."

 

Tyelknowle decided to take a friendly approach. "Look, Ulhero, there are people you report to. Who in this group do you not want to upset, who are the group's leaders."

 

Ulhero tells them, "I only know of two people. Dornen Finestone and Ilmeth Waelvur. Ilmeth is who I report to and he reports to Dornen. Dornen is our leader. We wear cloaks when we attend meetings so I don't know anyone else."

 

The heroes felt they heard enough from Ulhero and let him go. At the same time Armond was coming back into the tavern. The heroes talked about what to do next. Some talked about finding potions, others questioned this believer conspiracy, and they also brought up the missing delegation.

 

Who are these believers ? Who was this Earth Mage? Where did he go? What about this delegation that never came through? How many dice crashes James Video Card?
