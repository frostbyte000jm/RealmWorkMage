Mud-Bloods

Overview

Joclyn: She is an Elder of the Mud-Bloods.

Ander: He is a ranger that goes on missions with Joclyn.

Otho: He is a rogue that goes on missions with Joclyn.

Incidently, the Mud-bloods are named such because they are a collection of half breeds. Think theives caravan of dozens of mixed races. Every example you can think of and pretty much every class. They have ties in and out of Waterdeep and other major cities on the SWORD coast . A family more then a guild. Adopted family most often, but family still. And very loyal.

Ty has been away from the Mud-Bloods for some time. You will be getting history of your characters tribe (if someone has a better word let me know). This is why Ty spent a lot of time in cities looking for this tribe and eventually found them in the High Forest.
