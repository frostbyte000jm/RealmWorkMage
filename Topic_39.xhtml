Game 31

Detailed Entry

The heroes head south to explore the pool of water. In the middle was a yellow casket. Etched on the side is "Here rests Godwyd the Wyrmbane, dragonslayer and peacekeeper."

 

The casket contains dust, bones, a cloven skull and helm, and a battered breastplate. A gleaming shortsword is clutched in the skeletons hands. There was also a bag of 600 gold… “was…” Rowena remembered while tapping her pouch on her hip.

 

The heroes head North where they come across a deep cavern. There is a tiny ledge that one person at a time can make their way across. The difficulty is the wind. It has a mind of its own and it doesn’t wish anyone to cross.

 

After some discussion, the heroes tied themselves up and attempted to make their way across. After a couple of slips. “SLIPS?” Grimble recalls, “I slammed into the wall twice.”

 

“If it wasn’t for Rowena I would have gone off the side too!” Leokas said.

 

“Thank goodness you crawled” Barikhol said

 

“Not True! I didn’t crawl. Tell him Leokas” Grimble says.

 

“I literally couldn’t see anything.” Leokas replied.

 

Once across they were attacked by nine cloaked figures. As the cult members were standing up Rowena skips over there and blast them with cone of cold.

 

Eight of them shatter, and 1 of them is pinned to the ground.

 

Traveling a little more north, they run into the guards. Two Bariguras. These are large orange and blue ape like creatures.

 

Once they fight their way through them, they entered into the summoning room. This is where the heroes battle the god Yan-C-Bin.
