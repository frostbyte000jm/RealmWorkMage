Bastian

Overview

Real Name: Bastian Thermander

Gender: Male

Race: GenasiFire

Class: None

Age: Adult

Character Role: Adversary

Profile

Face of: A member of the Cult of the Eternal Flame .

Motivation: Like many in the fire cult , Bastian burns with an inner fire, but his fire is ambition, rather than a wish to see the world burn. Bastian is scheming to supplant Vanifer and claim Tinderstrike for himself.

As a practitioner of the arcane arts who learned much of his fire magic from Vanifer herself, Bastian relies on his spells in a fight, and he is a quick burn sort who tries to deliver maximum impact early in the fight. If he knows a fight is coming but cant preemptively strike, Bastian becomes more cautious, casting wall of fire to protect himself before he hurls magic into the fray.

Story

Interactions: Rowena 's backstory is tied to an adventuring party that was hunting him. Rowena is the only alive member of that party. Odert lives inside Rowena , and also remembers him.
