Heroes of Red Larch

Overview

The Heroes got this name through stories. In the Town of Red Larch there were nearby bandits, evil necromancers, and the town was building a religious cult. The protagonist of this story stepped in and helped Red Larch in its time of need. They became known in the Dessarin Valley as The Heroes of Red Larch.
