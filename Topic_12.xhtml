Game 04

Game Day

Game Date: 10/15/15

Date in Play: (4) Tarsakh 22, 1491, (4) Tarsakh 23, 1491

TLDR

  • The heroes were woken up by Owl Bears. They fought and killed them.
  • They traveled back towards Red Larch
  • On the road they meet Grinarv (a Dwarf from Barikhol ’s past)
  • They got back to town.
  • They talked about the different symbols of the cults of Air, Fire, and Earth.
  • Armond went and had fliers made up to find who the body is.
  • Kaylessa paid the heroes.
  • Wyer told the story of their Necromancer adventure.

Detailed Entry

This story started with the heroes sitting around the campfire. Rowena finished telling about her adventure with the group that didn't get a chance to avenge Princesses Francesca. The heroes that didn’t know Rowena introduced themselves.

 

They decided the safest course of action was to camp at the cave for the rest of the night, then head back to town after a full nights rest.

 

Before the evening ended Armond went back into the cave and wrapped up the necromancers body. He found sheets laying around in the necromancer’s chambers.

 

During the night, while Armond was on watch a creature lurked around outside the cave. Armond went out to take a look and saw an animal about the size of a fox. Armond noticed his eyes had a reflection of a flame. The creature ran off back into the night.

 

Rowena took 3rd watch. During her watch she heard something loud outside. It sounds like there is more than one. She runs over to wake up Barikhol . The Dwarf gets up and hears the creatures coming this way. He decides to wake everyone up and takes a peek outside.

 

Two Owl bears approach the cave. Barikhol informs the group there are two Owl Bears approaching. He turns back and throws an Axe landing it in the creatures shoulder.  As soon as the creature enters the light, arrows and spells release to strike the bear. He continues toward Barikhol , the one who hit the Owl Bear with an axe.

 

The fight continued with the 5 heroes fighting the two Owl Bears. At one point during the fight Barikhol was struck with the Owl Bears beak. He could see in his eyes a shape. It was some kind of triangle. He didn't have long to process this, because he was picked up and slammed to the ground. Barikhol was seriously injured.

 

Another moment, Armond was bit and the owl bear slammed him down with both claws. He pounded Armond to the earth. It let out a screech of victory. Armond was eyes were closing, but he too was able to see this shape flash through the creature’s eyes before Armond went to sleep.

 

Josephus , Wyer , and Rowena continued to fight the owl bears. Rowena casted her sleep spell "Go to sleep, go to sleep, please please please, GO TO SLEEP!" The bears fell to the ground. Josephus made sure the sleep was permanent.

 

Armond woke up four hours later. Everyone talked and they decided they should continue camping and leave once they are all well rested.

 

The next morning they all wake up, got ready, and ate a couple of rations. Armond , after almost dying from being mauled by an Owl Bear just 8 hours ago, stood up and threw the body of the necromancer over his shoulder. He was ready to go back to town. In the middle of the 6 hour trek, the heroes discovered someone on the side of the road. The dwarf that was badly injured. He was beaten, stabbed, lashed, and his eye was removed. Barikhol recognized him as a dwarf from his childhood. His name is Grinarv . He was cruel to Barikhol when he was younger. He called him things like Bad-Ale, based on his father's failing business.

 

Barikhol hinted as much to the rest of the heroes. They helped Barikhol tie up a splint and they carried him into town.

 

On entering the town, Barikhol and Wyer went to the temple. Armond went to see the constable. Rowena and Josephus went to see the bath house.

 

Barikhol and Wyer meet with Imdarr the priest of Tempus and he gave the Dwarf a healing potion. They also got him to the back room to clean up his wounds (GM sarcasm: If he just sleeps for the night apparently he will be fine… right?).

 

Armond slapped the dead body down on the Constable's work desk (btw: he works in a meat house. He was probably cleaning this counter for an hour after Armond left). Harburk gave Armond information where he can get some photos made by Endrith and reminded him that Kaylessa is probably the person to see about Lance Rock .

 

Armond left his place of employment and went down the Endrith . He slapped the Skull down on his desk. "Is that real?" Endrith asked. Armond told him this is the skull that was anchored to the tree with the arrow, which is still attached. Armond request that Endrith draws him up a photo sketch of this man, so he can try and get information on him. He pays Endrith 5 gold to do such.

 

Endrith drops everything he is doing and starts on this right away. He makes a beautiful poster and creates 20 copies and gives them to Armond .

 

Armond heads back to the Constable Harburk and asks if he can get these delivered to nearby towns. Harburk thinks this is possible and tells Armond he will get his messengers on it.

 

Armond goes to Kaylessa 's employment at Swinging Sword . He drops the body on the table. "Oh dat body can knowt stay heere tonight." Armond explains this is the body of the necromancer from Lance Rock and he brought it to Kaylessa . She explained that she just wanted the adventurers to take care of the fell magic and not go around and killing people like some assassins.

 

Armond frustrated by the interaction left without collecting payment and threw the body into the quarry. In the quarry there was a man working on breaking up some stones found in the mine. As he struck the rock a body wrapped up in a blanket fell beside him. He looks up and sees no one standing there.

 

Rowena went and bought new cloths from Haeleeya and requested her to start a bath. Rowena cleaned herself up and changed. She then left to join everyone at the tavern.

 

By this time just about everyone is in the Tavern. Wyer went to visit Haeleeya and then after to go back to visit with Imdarr .

 

In the Tavern, the heroes were enjoying their meals and ale. When they saw a familiar face. Tyelknowle was sitting a few booths away. Both the party and Ty noticed each other.

 

Armond started a conversation about something he saw in the Owl Bears eyes. It was a symbol. Barikhol noticed the same symbol. They started talking about the symbols they have been seeing lately. They started drawing them.

 

Josephus recognized the symbol that was in the Owl Bears eye. It was the symbol of the Air Cultest. Armond thinks the symbol that he saw years ago on the Goblins was a branding of the Fire Cult . Barikhol thinks the symbol tattooed on the neck of the dwarf was the symbol of Earth.

 

Armond decided to explain the visions he had when he was knocking on deaths door. He talked with a… benefactor… and he explained there was magic that wasn't of this world here. He explained it pretty much like he saw it in his dream. Most magic is generic. It exist in all worlds. But there is some magic of every world that once it crosses world it stops working. Think of magic like a child's block set. Most magic is circular and every world has a circle hole. Some worlds will also have a second hole that will be a square one world and a triangle in another.

 

The magic that has come to this world isn't of this world, but somehow it works. The magic is so powerful that the great beings of this world can't even sense it.

 

Partly during this conversation Barikhol and Rowena started talking about Tyelknowle . "Isn't that the guy that rescued the demon bandit? I am not sure how I feel about him right now." Barikhol said.

 

Rowena "Well I guess it would depend on what happened after. The Elf promised me that she will be dealt with, and I trusted him."

 

Barikhol said "I guess, but maybe I should keep an eye on him."

 

"If you want to keep an eye on him, great." Rowena started, “but may I suggest that you only keep an eye on him when we are not in combat. I mean I guess it would be ok when are doing that Monk Combat Meditation Ritual thing you do when we are fighting. Oh! It is time for my bath. I have to go.” Rowena leaves the Tavern.

 

Tyelkknowle takes a seat at the table, “I can’t help but notice that you are concerned with me and what happened with Joclyn those many years ago. There was a lot of confusion that day so I want to try and explain. Joclyn was a sister of mine from a group that are kind of like Travlers. She was 15 years old and she fell in love with a very bad man. This man was the leader of the highwaymen that attacked your caravan. Kids make stupid decisions, and I was there to try and help rescue her from her mistakes. When I took her back to the group she was severely dealt with, but I no longer tour with the Mud-Bloods . Does this help clear some things up?”

 

Barikhol , who has had a few glasses of ale by now says, “Ty! You sure know how to pick them.” and stands up to leave.

 

Kaylessa comes into the Tavern and meets Barikhol before leaving. “ Armond told me what you all did. I wanted to thank you. Armond left without me being able to give him the coins I have been trying to collect form towns people this week.” She drops the coins in front of Armond . “Thank you again. People need to hear your story. Who can tell it?” The group all decided that Wyer would be the best person to tell it.

 

Armond , “Maybe next time you can give more specific instructions.” Kaylessa left the tavern kind of hurt.

 

For the next week the heroes have been doing their own things . About one week later then heroes are sitting in the Tavern when they all hear a rumble and a loud crash outside…
