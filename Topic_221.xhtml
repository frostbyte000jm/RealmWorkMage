TLDR

  • Rowena was taken by the Cult of the Crashing Wave.

Detailed Entry

Due to feedback I received I am braking the story up into two stories. One story will have "For Rowena" the other story will have "For Everyone Else". You may choose to read both stories and I am ok with that. I have faith that you can separate story from character knowledge.

For Everyone Else

 

The adventure started with Tyelknowle returning to Red Larch meeting up with the others over breakfast.

 

A lot needed to be talked about.

1) The heroes taking this side adventure to retrieve back the boats for Teria and Sesa.

2) Armond agreeing to work with a necromancer to clear out the Earth Cult temple.

3) Why they are going to go after the boat instead of trying to take down the earth cult?

 

Teria and Sesa joined in on this conversation. At some point Grimble told them, you should continue to Waterdeep to find people to handle this task for you.

 

Teria replied "That is your answer? Why did we waste time with you all last night then? It would have been much better if you told us this answer before the caravan left!" She stood up kicking the chair over and started to storm out of the Helm at Highsun.

 

Barikhol jumped up to pause her, "Hold on. Let us work this out. We will help you get back your boat."

 

Grimble and Tyelknole do not agree with working with a Necromancer. Even to the point of using him. Necromancy is evil as far as society is concerned. Most towns wouldn't need any justification to kill a necromancer.

 

The argument is that the necromancer could help get what they need from the earth cult, and he is only asking for his knife as payment.

 

Grimble believes they should be going after the Cult of the Black Earth instead of moving on to the Cult of the Crushing Wave. Tyelknowle seems to agree on this. Armond and Barikhol said they are not ready to return to the Sacred Stone Monastery.

 

Grimble made some kind of remark that Barikhol screwed up the attack on the Earth Cult last time and Barikhol just about lost it. At last moment Barikhol was able to keep his composure and walked out of the Helm at Highsun.

 

Barikhol was walking around not really paying attention when he found himself in their room. He saw that all of Rowena's stuff was gone and a note left on the bed.

 

Friends, I am sorry. I know you needed me for tomorrow, but something is wrong with me and I need answers before it gets worse. I know you will be heading to the water cult location. I will look for you there, but if you go elsewhere send word to Red Larch and I will find you. I am so sorry, I love you guys but I am a liability right now. See you soon, I hope.​

 

This made Barikhol even angrier. He went back into the tavern and slammed the note back on the table.

 

The discussion end and they heroes made way towards the Water Cult.

 

(Storyteller Note here: in all the confusion I forgot that the two sisters were traveling along with you. They would have totally joined into this scene. Pretend they did.)

 

That night during Armond's watch, he took a few steps away because he thought he heard something. He noticed on the ground was running water and it was moving unlike water would.

 

"Everyone wake up!" Armond screamed. They did and they drew their weapons.

 

There was a Priest, a Water Genasi, and a handfull of fighters who were there to attack the group.

 

The Priest went down quickly and the Water Genasi took a couple hard hits and went invisible. After seeing the other drop so quickly the fighters ran.

 

Armond threw an improbable eldritch blast to hit one before he escaped. They were able to capture and keep one alive.

 

Tyelknowle and Grimble asked "Everyone ok?" they started healing the group.

 

Armond asks "How did the water cult know we were out here?"

 

Everyone looked around and eventually their eyes landed on the sisters.

For Rowena

 

Rowena steps into her room. Something felt off. Everything in her body told her to run, but she couldn't. Rowena looks down as she feels something cold and wet on her. Its water, but she can't push through it like water.

 

Rowena trying to figure out what is going on looks around the room. All her stuff is packed up. On the bed is a note. she follows the water. It goes behind her to the corner. It is a large snake like body made purely of water.

 

Rowena struggles to break free, but she is restrained. She starts to scream, but the water quickly moves over her face and Rowena can't breathe. She realizes she is drowning. It is quiet. Everything is quiet. Her vision starts to blur, and then the room goes dark.

 

"Why can't we kill her?" one man says

"Brother, I told you. Grimjaw wants her as insurance. These heroes of Red Larch are starting to make a name for themselves." another man that sounds a lot like the first man replied.

 

Rowena is tied up. She can feel her arms bound in front of her and the rope goes around her neck. Rowena tries a little trick using her prestidigitation to untie the rope but the moment she went to whisper the words and flick the fingers, the magic felt suffocated. Using her Arcana knowledge, she can tell this is some kind of magical rope that mutes casters.

 

Rowena could tell she could talk just fine, but the moment she would try magic, it stopped. You would think Rowena would be a little frustrated by her situation, but this rope is actually kind of cool.

 

Her face had a bag over it. She couldn't really see. She could hear voices and movement, but she couldn't make out anything. She knew she was outside. She could feel her Fox nudge her. It was a strange feeling. It was like real, but not at the same time.

 

"It's ok. We will figure something out." she whispered to the fox.

 

"Will you now?" a voice replies. "Your frie..." his sentence was cut short by the sound of a man ridding up quickly on horseback. At least Rowena assumed it was a horse.

 

"The heroes of Red Larch have left." a new voice says. "They are on their way up the Larch Path."

 

"Let’s go get them." a man says. The group gives words of agreement.

 

"If we do this, we shall go with our god Olhydra" an older man says, the group says a chant. “To the depths and back.”

 

It sounded like everyone left. So Rowena maneuvered herself to remove her mask and shake her hair from her face. As soon as her eyes focused she was staring at a rather thick dark blue man.

 

"Brave." He said, "You see the mask was for your protection. We would have rather you not seen our faces." He pulls out a knife, sharpens it, and then pulls out an apple and starts cutting it. "What is in this for you?"...

 

((I actually would like to play this out. We can do it between games. But there is some conversation here I am curious where it would go.))

 

After the talk finishes he tells Rowena, "I won't be too far. I would say don't try anything, but I think you can figure out moving from that spot would not go well."

 

Rowena actually didn't figure that out. She just now noticed that she wasn't sitting on the ground. She was on some kind of plate.

 

Once he leaves Rowena felt a familiar feeling bubbling up in her. Her instincts told her to suppress it, but then she saw her fox. Staring straight at her with one of those k9 grins.

 

The Water Genasi comes running back and so does the others.

 

"Not now... Must wait for the right moment." Rowena told herself. Suppressing has been difficult and this took some coaxing, but Rowena finally held it down.

 

"Brother what happen." the Genasi with the apple said.

 

"Reash. They... They killed Drosnin." he replied. "Take these men and head to the rondeaux. I will bring the girl."

 

"But you're hurt." Reash said.

 

"It is a girl. She is suppressed. What she going to do?" he replied.

 

Reash didn't like it but he followed his brother’s orders.

 

The brother started doing things to the camp. Rowena watched and realized that he was covering up their trail. The camp was starting to look old, maybe a few weeks.

 

"It's time to go my half human." the Genasi started to reach down for her and Rowena could hear the yip of a fox.

 

She let go. She let go of everything that she has been suppressing. She let it out. Wild magic is strongly connected to a mages emotions. Rowena realized that she hadn't been just suppressing her magic, but her emotions as well. She had been trying to stay strong for her spirit, for her friends, for Odert, and she was realizing just now, for herself.

 

Rowena let it all out yelling, screaming, crying, laughing... with these emotions came the wild magic. The world slowed down. Rowena could see she was moving twice as fast as before. She could feel her skin soften like it was as weak as paper.

 

The Water Genasi felt the same. "What is this? How is this happening?" He started looking around because he could also hear music playing nearby. The Genasi looks over at Rowena and down at her bonds. "You can't be doing this."

 

"But I think I am." she replied. A long beard of feathers grew on her face. "Just once I would like to say a line and my magic make me look bad ass. Is that really too much to ask?" She looks over at her fox and watches his head tilt. Then Rowena braced for being hit with iron skillet. When she opened her eyes Rowena wasn't tied up anymore. She was standing 60 feet away looking back watching the plate trap go off in the genasi's face. Electric eels came flying out biting and shocking the genasi. He didn't stand a chance.

 

Rowena went running back in the direction from where the others came. She knew the hills, she would just need to get her bearings.

 

"mi lass, look up." Odert said "I can help wid dis one."

 

Rowena did as he said and she felt her arm raise up and point. "Dis way."

 

She started running again.

 

Rowena heard the voices she wasn't sure what they were talking about, but she knew who they were.
