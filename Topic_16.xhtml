Detailed Entry

(Sorry, I am really off my game and have been super busy. These notes are probably going to be shorted versions as well)

 

The story started as the adventurers were coming into a town called Ospit. The town was a little dilapidated and didn’t seem to have many visitors for it being on such a main road. They approached a man wearing a coat. The coat could have once been a perfectly clean white coat, but it is showing it's years. He welcomes the heroes to the town "Welcome travelers, how may our towns people assist you?"

 

The heroes pointed out that the town seems to have no visitors. The man replied, "Ahhh yes. We were once a thriving town. This was more of a research and aid center, but our services are not needed anymore. For years scholars have traveled here to study medicine, surgery, and all sorts of mundane and magical healing. But ever sense the law of 5 came about, we are no longer needed in the world."

 

When asked about the law of 5 he replied, "The law of 5…"

 

Another interrupts from the distance, "Don't forget about subsection e."

 

"Right," he continues "The law of 5e undid all of our hard work and now adventurers can do in a nights rest what they use to adventure here because they were in need of services."

 

The heroes suggested that they adapt to the new law, "Oh we have. Some of our people who have been working in the field for years are now mere potion brewers. We are having a sale on our potions, 20gp a bottle."

 

He heroes bought up some supplies and took rest in the town for the night. (Erin, your character may have made some potion purchases at this time. Just let me know at the start of next game.)

 

The next day the heroes arrived in Beliard . They started asking the local towns people about the delegation and they had some wild theories. Ty went to speak with the local church. It was a lady of sune by the name of Victoria she told him what she knew about the delegation, how they headed south to stop by Summit Hall because one their knights died and he was from that town. He had major burns from some kind of creature.

 

Josephus , Armond , Rowena and Barikhol went to the local tavern to speak with others. Armond started asking questions to the bar maid and she told him what she had heard. They went toward Summit Hall but never made it there. She knows this because a traveler from Summit Hall said they never made it and he didn't pass them on the road either. The heros quickly noticed that the town has a twag dialect that can sometimes be hard to understand. Barikhol had no problem falling right back into this accent.

 

Since Ty wasn't there Barikhol wanted to bring up how he has been feeling about Ty.

 

Another patron of the tavern told the heroes to speak with Eann (Sorry if I gave another name, whatever I gave I can go with that, but this was the name I had written) about what he saw out on the Dessarin Road.

 

The heroes take his advice and head down to Eann 's Apple Grove, passing Rowena on the way. When they arrived at Eann farm he asks "er ye flla lost?" The heroes informed him that they are looking for Eann .

 

"Oy, dat be may. Wut can I dou fur ya?" he asked

 

Barikhol jumped in, "we be needen sum help, ther be a delegation travlin down the Dessarin Road n dey seemed ta disappear. I hear ye be knowin sumt'in aboot dis."

 

Eann replies "ay, I has, it be nice to hear trav'las who talkin proper. Der ha been attalks on the Dessarin Road I sau wid me own eyes. A large bird swooped out of de sky n pulcked a travla off the rud." Eann buddy claimed he was a drunkin fool, but Eann refuted this and said it was true.

 

The heroes asked a few more clarifying questions about the bird and where it happened, and if they knew to whom. The farmer was able to answer where, but didn't know much about the bird and who it was. No one claimed the missing person.

 

He heroes meet up, trade information, and head down the road. On the way they see vultures flying about a mile off the trail. They decided to go investigate. Once they get over there they find the wreckage of what could have been the delegation. Going through the dead bodies they saw soldiers with the black coats and red axes. Barikhol knew it was the guards of mirabar.

 

There was one still alive. He was badly injured. They pulled him from the wreckage and bandaged him up. This is when they heard the screech of a giant vulture. Three beast came flying out of the skys and down towards the heroes. A fight broke out. Armond started his eldritch blast, Barikhol pulled out his throwing axes, Josephus pulled out his bow, Rowena summoned her sorcery, and Ty pulled out his bow.

 

There were many trades during the battle, but two of the highlights where when Ty ripped the cape off the soldier and threw it over the bird and prepared to give a lift to Barikhol to land on it.

Also when Barikhol got swept up into a grapple from the air elemental and it turned out doing this was a big mistake.

 

After the fight the heroes took the air elemental and the survivor back to town.
