Primium

Overview

Description: It was first discovered in a different world. Primium is a metal made from alchemically purified silver and gold. It retains the mystical properties of both of those metals to harm changing breeds harmed by such metals, and it also contains anti-magical properties to cancel supernatural or magical attacks and defenses. Very few Mage groups have the knowledge to be capable of creating the expensive metal.
