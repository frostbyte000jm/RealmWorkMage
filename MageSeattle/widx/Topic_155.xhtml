Vic left Teddy ’s office to go check out Douglass Truth Branch Public Library . When he got there, he saw a disturbing scene with windows blown out. Vic heard a horrible crunching inside along with gargled screams.

Running inside he saw a mini tornado twisting an older woman completely from head to toe. It turns to him and whispers something in a language Vic cannot understand. “What does it mean ‘you’re not on my list?’” Dee asks.

“Son of a Bitch! You’re still here?” Vic responded a little startled.

“Yeah, but only… LOOK OUT!” Dee shouted for Vic to move.

Vic dives out of the way of a flying bookshelf. He draws a symbol and unwinds his hands attempting to move the forces counter to the way the spirit is acting. It seems to be working, until the tornado zaps Vic with lightning.

Vic throws up a shield to protect himself and it held… mostly. He is a little singed and knows he will be feeling that in the morning. “Any Ideas?” Vic yells at Dee .

“Yes.” Vic hears from the other side of the room. It is a very familiar voice. This voice pops up from time to time in his dreams, and usually they are not good ones. He turns to see his blonde-haired apprentice Evangeline . She hasn’t changed… much. She is wearing two very old and very distinct bracelets.

She chants some words in a language that brings nauseating horror to Vic ’s stomach and releases a force that slams the spirit back into the ceiling and onto the second floor. “Here!” she says tossing Vic a bracelet.

Vic is a trained, well educated, hermetic mage. He knows better than to fall for these tricks and blocks the bracelet with a book. “These things will possess you!”

“Only if you’re a weak minded…” She looks him up and down, shrugs, then runs up the stairs. “That one is for Forces! What happen to Research Research Research?”

 “… I didn’t have time, and this isn’t my area of expertise!” Vic muttered back at her as he started up the stairs.

“There were more than just the middle ages you know!” she snipped back at him. It really felt like old times. But Vic knew it wasn’t like old times at all.

“Also… how did she hear me?” Vic thinks.

She had no reason to lie to him, or did she? Vic hated that he was about to do this, but he really has no choice. He doesn’t know anything about Spirits. “Will this allow me to blast a ghost?”

“Man… Fuck you!” Dee shouted back at him.

“It was mostly made for Spirits! Why are you having Ghost problems? Can’t you request some Ghost Dust from your Chantry ?” She hollers back as she is blasted backwards and almost starts to fall down the stairs.

Vic reaches out and catches her as she stumbles back. They pause for a moment remembering the last time they were in a situation like this. She gives him a side smirk and tells him “You’re in my shot.” Vic sees her quick gestures and knows what is coming. He spins out of the way and ducks low as her ball of dark matter builds on her palm. He backs it up by creating concentrated flames that start building around the spirit to remove the oxygen.

The Spirit lets out a loud roar. The building starts to shake and the ground beneath them caves. They begin to fall, and she releases the blast. Vic opening up a gap large enough to let the blast in. It explodes as Vic and Evangeline hit the ground.

Vic coughs from the debris but is able to sit up. “Owe.” He exclaims.

“Shit.” Evangeline says with a strained grunt.

Vic looks over and sees that she was impaled by wrought iron through the stomach.

“FUCK FUCK FUCK FUCK FUCK FUCK FUCK!” Vic yells as he rips her torn jeans to tie off the wound.

“What the hell was that?” another familiar voice is heard. Vic looks up to see Ev the officer. “Help me get her out of here, this place is unsafe.”

Vic nods and helps carry Evangeline out of the library with Ev. They get outside where it is safe and gently lay her down. Ev pulls out her phone and starts to make a call… “NO!” Evangeline grabs her arm “They will kill me if they find me.”

“What the hell are you into now Victor?” Ev says.

“Ummm” Vic responds with superiority.

“Who is this?” Evangeline asks.

“Well…” Vic replies clearly the leader of this situation.

“I am none of your concern.” Ev says to Evangeline . Vic seems a little surprised by this reply. Ev says “ Vic you need to come clean. Who are you working for? Why did you set me up?”

“Wait… What?” Vic clearly needs to get a handle on this situation.

“Why did you set me up?” Ev asks again.

“I have no clue what you’re talking about.” Vic says. “I just told you what I knew, if it lead you to a witches house, that isn’t my fault.”

“wait.. what?” Ev says

“what?” Vic distracts.

“What agency do you work for?” She asks him

“It’s some acronym that you may not have heard of.” Vic replies sarcastically

“Before today, I would have chuckled at that.” Ev starts, “But now…”

“Look, clearly I am behind. What happened? What is going on?” Vic asks

“I took that information you gave me to my Captain. He took one look at it and said ‘Don’t worry about it.’ I tried to push, but the best I got was he put a couple uniforms on it. They are not detectives.” She says “Then I followed up on that video I showed you. I know you killed DeAngelo. So, I went to the DA’s office to get a warrant.”

“Fuck” Vic says looking at her hand on the gun.

“Well,” Ev begins, “You do not have to worry. Some lady in a fancy suit showed up, said she worked for a government agency and you work for her. She told the DA that she should not file charges. Do you know what was weirder? The DA said OK! No one has that power in this country, how do you?”

“Have you ran a background check on me?” Vic asks.

“Yeah. But I don’t have access to it anymore because I am no longer reporting for duty. What the fuck did I do to you?” Ev replies.

Vic notices the gun Officer Ev is carrying isn’t a standard issue “Can you describe the agent to me? Like someone out of the XFiles?”

“Maybe.” Ev admits “She is Asian, carries a cane, but uses it only periodically, black hair, could be long, but it was tightly wrapped. Do you know her?”

“Not yet, but I know of them.” Vic replies. “I am not working for them. They are after me. I’m sorry, but I think I introduced you to a world of shit.”

Vic listens sympathetically, “There is nothing I can do about that, and I am really sorry those things lead up to you losing your job. I can help you with a job, and I can help you with your financial situation.”

“Really? If these people are after you, how are you going to help me then?” Ev questions

“I have been at it for a very long time.” Vic explains. “This isn’t really anything new to me.”

“Why are they after you?” Ev starts going into investigation mode.

“Fuck you!” but she didn’t really mean it.

Vic tells her everything. Vampires , Werewolves , Magick, and more. He tells her that this world is worst than she could imagine.
