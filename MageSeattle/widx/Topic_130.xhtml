Vic pulls his gun, Xen gets her BUGs ready, Sani moves forward with his arms out, and Trent backs up into a corner.

“Is that a rope?” Sani asks.

“Fuck…” Trent says as he realizes his 100-foot nylon rope is missing from his shadow room. He now remembers tying himself off with it on a dock in Ella ’s subconscious. After they drowned they were in a different scene and he sort of, kind of, forgot about it.

Trent tries to pull the rope and Ella cries out. He stops and lets Teddy look. Teddy gets to work trying to help.

It could be a glitch, but it unquestionably stood out before it went away. “Well she is good with the mind, and she can move through space.” Trent explains.

“Yes, those gifts are wonderful…” Snider begins, “but why can she do them? I hope my words are not offensive, you are a Sahajiya, but you are a rarity in our group. We are masters of time, has she shown time manipulation? We would love to increase our members, but we should think about her first.”

Trent takes a look and pulls the pill out to take a LOOK at it. On touching it Snider flinched a little. Trent uses his mage sight to see closer and can tell the pill is made up of some kind of magical properties, but it doesn’t mean it is mage magick. “What does it do?”

“It is pretty wild.” He said “They are calling it Lifestyle, it makes you feel an experience.”

Trent goes to hand it back and Snider puts up his hand “That one is yours.”

Trent thinks to himself ‘So, Snider is a germaphobe.’

Sani tries looking more into the girl. He stakes out her house, tries the google, and more. He isn’t getting anything new, but he starts to get used to his flipped entropy feelings. Sani starts a spell outside her house to sense life inside. It is like looking though smoke. He can tell the girl is inside, but not picking up anything else.

He reaches down and grabs some dirt, and rubs his fingers together to let it fall back down. He takes is middle finger and touches it to his tongue and he can see more in the house. So much is reviled. He can see tables, chairs, stairs, walls and more that make up the house. He can also see two bodies that used to be life, but now are clumps of matter laying on the bed in what he assumes is the master bedroom.

“You know this feels weird.” Xenori replies.

Xen starts logging a digital trail of everything she can in the house. It appears that the bodies have been lying there for at least two months. The recording didn’t show how they died, but just that they have been in the bed for that long.

The girl lives a very routine life. She comes home, says “Hello mother, hello father”, does some chores, watches TV, and goes to bed. Xen can see her when she is watching TV. it is the same time for about 2 hours every night. She turns on the TV and it plays static, like you would get on old tube televisions.
