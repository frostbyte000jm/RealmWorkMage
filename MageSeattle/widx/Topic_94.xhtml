Name:: Sani

Backstory

Sani was born upon a Navajo reservation in New Mexico.  At his adolescence, he attended the annual “Rise of the Nations” event in Arizona.  He met with members of several tribes, imbibed Peyote, and ended up high as hell with members of the Mandan tribe.  The Mandans were one of the original peoples to use suspension as a form of enlightenment (along with neo-stone age Indians south of Tibet).  With the frame of mind that Sani was in, Suspension sounded great. 

                Sani damn near died.  Between the Peyote and Suspension, he begun to hallucinate; although the hallucinations were actually and positively real!  There was no doubt in Sani’s mind.  Sani was approached by a coyote, which was laughing.  The laughter was contagious, and Sani found this hilarious!  At this point the coyote began to stand on his hind legs and dance, so Sani did as well.  This was all great fun.

                The next day, Sani awoke in a Teepee belonging to a Sioux Medicine Man.  The Sioux asked him if he saw Coyote, and Sani realized that he danced with the Navajo Trickster God “Coyote”.  Since that time Coyote has evolved and may arrive in Sani’s mind as a coyote, as a coyote man hybrid, or as “Coyote” the Trickster God (which looks a lot like T-Hawk from the old Street Fighter Games”.  Coyote always has “ADVICE” as opposed to advice, which tends to portent things in reality in very strange ways.

                Over the next several years, Sani grew into a speaker for the United Tribes, which really paid off in television ads paid for by state and federal agencies with the “Rise of the Nations” organization being paid a percentage of money from the ads.  Sani became well known after working closely with producers and tribes to bring about this commercial ( https://youtu.be/j7OHG7tHrNM ).

                During this time, Sani’s relationship with Coyote developed.  Coyote is a devious trickster, and Sani is really not sure if Coyote is responsible for the scams he started running, or if these personality traits naturally developed.  Sani developed two strong personalities/aliases; Abbas Oman and Vihaan Reyansh.  Sani has used these two so frequently in scams and cons that he can slip into either character with a heartbeat.  Life magick also helps with the identity changes! (beard length, eye, and hair color, not to mention defining characteristics like moles and birthmarks that throw off most recognition).

Abbas often wears a white thawb and keffiyeh and sports a long salt and pepper beard.  He often refers to his “exalted master”, which he claims is a rich sheik, but will never mention his name.

                Vihaan dresses as an Indian born in the mother country that moved to America at a young age.  He can play a more traditional role, or can be more Americanized.

                Sani tends to work towards the betterment of the people, and directly against anyone that tries to keep the people in the dark in regards to magick and the “Truth”.  The “Truth” that Sani so greatly believes in is the great wheel.  To quote Robert Jordan “ The Wheel of Time turns, and Ages come and pass, leaving memories that become legend. Legend fades to myth, and even myth is long forgotten when the Age that gave it birth comes again.”

                If Sani and Coyote are correct, and the wheel moves faster, father can teach son, then when the father dies and is reborn, son can teach father.  This will bring about Ascension for all of The People.  Sani must determine people and things that slow and try to stop the wheel, and as Ethanatoi, give those people and things an opportunity for rebirth and redemption (Sani really opposes looking at this as outsiders do; it’s not about death).
