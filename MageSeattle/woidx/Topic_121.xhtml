Game 05 Trent

Game Notes

Trent sits in his shadow room and focuses on expanding his conscious to try and find the lost girl. Ella Heart. Out of the shadows pops a mage. Trent jumps up to prepare himself. Until now Trent has never seen another mage use shadow walking before. He goes to attack the intruder but gets knocked on his ass. He looks over to where he was just sitting, and he is still sitting there. “what the fuck?”

The mage turns around and it is… well to say it was him would be misleading. It is someone that looks like him, but more experienced. “Are you from the future?”

“God! You are such a nerd.” Puck says from the lips of experienced Trent . “No. Stop thinking this is and episode of Heroes. You are trying to tap into something new that you never done before and I am here to keep you from dying.”

“What is this?” Trent asks as this is a new experienced for him.

“This is your mind realm.” Puck says. “You haven’t quite figured it out so it is a little week, but if I was you, I would really get used to this place.” Trent reaches for the meditating version of himself and pulls from his front pockets a pair of eyeglasses. Putting them on he can see the use of Correspondence, Mind, and something else here.

“what is that?” Trent asks.

“You haven’t figured it out yet.” Puck says “Look, what you’re trying to do will kill you without a sympathetic link. You need something to connect to her and I can help you do this.”

“Why are you helping me?” Trent asks used to Puck kind of being more of a dick.

“You’re finally trying.” He says as he pushes Trent back into his meditating body and he falls over.
