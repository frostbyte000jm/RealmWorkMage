Council of Nine Mystic Traditions

The Council of Nine Mystic Traditions, or simply the Traditions, are a collection of magical traditions united by a common theory of magical Spheres, as well as a common purpose: the defense of magic and myth against the oppressive, static and safe Technocratic paradigm of predictability and control.
