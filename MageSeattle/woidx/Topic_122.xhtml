Game 05 Xenori

Game Notes

Xenori heads over to West Bridge to meet with her friend Lucas.  He talks to her about the stabbing she saw on the video. “I saw her without her hat. She had pigtails, blonde I am pretty sure.” Xen talks to him some more about anything they need and the usual conversation.

She goes by the stand a couple times, but no one seems to be manning it. She tries to follow the path, but eventually loses track of the woman.

While walking back Xen is hit with a weird sensation, static. That is her best description. It overwhelms her senses. She has felt this one before, but the last time the volume was at 1 and now it feels like it is at 7. Using her BUGs to scan around the local area, she can see the breaks in reality. Other people in the area do not seem to see, feel, or hear this.

As soon as it came on, it went away.
