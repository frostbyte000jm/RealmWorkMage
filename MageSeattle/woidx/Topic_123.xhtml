Game Overview

Theme: Consensual Reality is Changing - Due to so many people subscribing to the belief of the Fake News, Flat Earthers, not believing in Climate Science, Vaccines cause autism, Holocaust never happened, and even more real things that have no grounding in reality, this is changing the Consensual Reality and causing glitches in magick. Sometimes Paradox happens with Coincidental Effects, but sometimes it doesn’t happen with Vulgar Effects. It is hard to predict, and the bubbles of shifting reality do not stay in one place for long. No one knows why this is happening in Seattle or how many other places it is happening.

Threat: The Technocracy are Relentless – Most places the Technocracy has been laying low and not bothering the Traditions for many years, but in recent years the Technocracy hasn’t been going out of their way to take out Reality Deviants, but when one “gets in their way” they have no problem removing the delinquent. The rise in attacks seems to not be officially sanctioned. The contacts within the Technocracy claim to know nothing about it and deny their involvement with the accused. Unfortunately, there isn’t a truce when mages give cause for other groups to attack, such as thievery, assault, divulging secrets, and more.

 

 

Keep in mind, with the Fronts below that some of them should be combined, and some of them only partially link up. As we continue this list will keep getting additional information added to it until the Front is completed.

Front 1

F1 - Title: The Breath-Taking Court Case

F1 - Description: There was a court case where a rich teenager by the name of Ethan Brewster, was driving under the influence. He crashed his car accidently killing a person by the name of Jakki Davis. In the last few weeks there has been a number of deaths by asphyxiation. It turns out, that the deaths are all members of the court case, all but 3.

 

Vic was looking into some possible magickal items for Susan. He found one at a pawn shop ran by a gang member named Dee. Vic retrieved the item, but Susan died also by asphyxiation. It wasn’t until later that she was connected to the court case.

 

Trent was walking down the street when a younger adult named Manel fell and almost landed on him. Since his apartment is right above Susan’s apartment, it is believed that these cases may be connected.

 

There was also an incident where the apartment right beneath Susan’s the tenant was taken to the hospital due to breathing issues.
