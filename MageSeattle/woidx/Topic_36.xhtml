Doissetep

Overview

Description: Doissetep, also referred to as the Fortress of the Aethyrs or Chantry of Cal Ladeim, was an Order of Hermes stronghold and one of the greatest chantries in Tradition history. Its fall was one of the portents that heralded the Avatar Storm and the Final Nights.
