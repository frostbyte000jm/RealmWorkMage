Vallivoe's Sundries

Overview

Outside: Aside from the bewildering profusion of doors, barrels, rotting old furniture, and tools leaning against its outside wall, this building looks like a private home. A small faded sign on the front of the door reads, “Vallivoe’s Sundries”

 

Inside: Rooms are crammed to the rafters with new wares and used items of all sorts.

 

Endrith Vallivoe sells new and used goods from his home. Almost anything might be available to buy here, buried under heaps of other stuff.
