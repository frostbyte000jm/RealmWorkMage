Elemental Cults

Overview

There are four cults; Cult of the Black Earth, Cult of the Crushing Wave, Cult of the Eternal Flame, and Cult of the Howling Hatred. They have been around for a long time, but it wasn’t until recently their activity has picked up on the sword coast.

 

Raids, robberies, kidnappings, murders, and weird weather events could probably be tied back to one of these four cults.

 

No one knows what they really want, many have guessed, but the protectors of the sword coast are having trouble tracking these cults down.
