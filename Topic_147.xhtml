Armond Collaborative Adventure

Stories

Heroes:: Armond , Josephus , Wyer

After many years as a wizard's apprentice Armond secretly made a pact with an entity becoming a warlock.  After Armond 's master found out Armond was banished from the wizard's tower out on to the streets. With nothing but the clothes on Armond 's back, he had to sleep in alleyways and steal for food. Several months had past and the future looking bleak Armond decided to travel the country side looking to find something new to life and expand his knowledge of the unknown.  Only problem was he had no money and no idea where to start.

 

While begging his way through a small town, Armond met a well-built man who offered to make him a meal.  The man's name was Josephus .   Armond followed Josephus to a camp outside of the town where there was a fire ready and waiting.   Armond was treated to a delicious, yet simple meal.  While they were eating and talking, the two were set upon by three goblins who were lured to the area by the aroma of the delicious food.

 

Wyer walked along the humble country road anticipating a hot meal and a warm bed after his long days travel. A short way outside the town that was his destination he noticed something unusual, goblin tracks. They crossed the muddy dirt road and passed into the woods to his right. Not willing to leave a potential threat alive this close to a peaceful town, he proceeded to followed them. Before reaching the end of the trail he was startled to hear shouts and the sounds of battle ahead. He rushed forward into a small camp and was greeted by the sight of two travelers being set upon by goblins. He joined the battle and together they quickly dispatched the offensive creatures.

 

Have dispatched the goblins and with a belly full of food Almond thanked the two men for the contributions. With little in the way of money Armond offered to travel with them and help them on their quests with the hope of collecting a little coin.
