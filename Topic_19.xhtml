TLDR

  • The heroes regrouped to discuss their options.
  • They decided fighting the Feathergale Knights was a bad idea, they better play along and see what they can get from Lord Commander Thurl Merosska.
  • Thurl gave the heroes information where The Cult of the Black Earth are hiding. It is a place called the Sacred Stone Monastery .
  • Thurl is hiding something.
  • The heroes traveled to the Monastery and setup watch.

Detailed Entry

The heroes came together in one of their rooms. Armond was inspecting the ring as Josephus and Barikhol was retelling the story of fighting the manticore.

 

After, Rowena and Tyelknowle caught up the team in what they found.

 

The general consensus of the party is they do not trust the Feathergale Knights . They feel the place is infiltrated by one of the elemental cults . The problem is, they are quite out numbered. Picking a fight with the Feathergale Knights and if they are bad, there are way more of them than the heroes. Their best bet is to go about their adventure and see if they can get anything from the Lord Commander Thurl Merosska.

 

The party agreed to this and got some sleep for the night.

 

The next morning the heroes woke up to the break of dawn and the smell of wonderful food. Coming out of their rooms they saw the Feathergale Knights working to prepare for breakfast.

 

One of the higher ranking knights was assigning duties. She looks over to the guest and asks "You joining us for breakfast?"

 

When the heroes nodded or spoke that they were, she tells them "Great, everyone in the spire has a job, even the guest." Then she assigns them some tasks to do. Josephus got to go work in the kitchen. Rowena got to go work with the hippogriffs. The rest of the heroes got assigned random duties.

 

Once food was prepared and out everyone came together and ate. The heroes noticed that most of the knights at the table, we unarmed. They were not even being cautious of their visitors anymore. The knights welcomed and initiated conversation. People told stories of their homes, politics, loved ones, and more.

 

After the meal, everyone went back to their duties. The Lord Commander asked the heroes to join him in his chambers.

 

Ty and Rowena noticed the letter was no longer visible when entering. The Lord Commanders chambers was pristine. Everything had a place and everything was perfectly clean. This is a soldier that takes great care of his living quarters and this may give some insight to his expectations of his fellow knight's quarters.

 

Merosska motions everyone to take a seat, there seems to be enough places for everyone. "It looks like you all are ready to get back out there. I want you to know there is always a welcomed room for you here at the spire." He entertains questions from the heroes.

The Lord Commander talks with the heroes on sharing knowledge about the cults. He is most disturbed when they mention the delegation was attacked by air. He tells the heroes that 5 of his men have gone missing and 2 of them so far have turned up naked and dead. He is afraid the air cult has robbed them and going around as Feathergale Knights . He mentions that he has a group already looking into that angle and hopefully will know more soon. [anyone skilled in Insight knows he is hiding something]

"… The place is called the Sacred Stone Monastery . What my spies have gathered is this place has been taken over by the The Earth Cult . There has been sightings of Mud Sorcerers entering this Monastery. If you learn anything about the Black The Earth Cult , please come back and share it with me so I can relay it to Waterdeep. They have taken a keen interest in this new cult rising since the attacks are growing more predominant."

 

Merosska draws a map for the heroes and sends them on their way. The Monastaery is hidden to the south east of the spire. It is roughly a full days walk. Once they arrived the heroes found a spot that they are far enough away to scout the monastery without being seen.

 

The heroes spend some time collecting information. During the day there seems to be more activity than at night. It is kind of hard to tell since most of the shutters on their windows are closed, but it is possible to see the glow of torches at night, and it seems very low on activity.

 

While setting up a place to camp, since they are going to be here for a little while, Barikhol takes a moment to give a teaching lesson to Armond . "Do ye smell that?"

 

Armond , "Aye. Is that a wine?"

 

Barikhol replies, "No, but I can see why ye think so. 'Tis brandy, but I doubt dey let der fruit distill long enough, which is what would make it smell like wine. Either dey are amateurs or dey are purposefully making it cheap."

 

Tyelknowle and Josephus went off to scout out the monastery. Ty counted 13 exterior windows that appeared locked, 4 doors, and possibly roof access. Josephus noticed they send guards on patrol every 45 minutes to 75 minutes.

 

The adventure will start with the heroes getting back together to tell each other what they have found.
