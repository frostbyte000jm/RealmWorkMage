Story

Barikhol is renting the space from Mother Yulantha. She had in her possession an old Brewery that has long been shut down. Barikhol helped cleaned up this place to make it a business.

While Barikhol has been gone Grinarv has been taking care of the place of business. Barikhol has made him officially apart of the staff.

Additional Details

·       Forefather's Fire Mead (aka: Magma Mead)

o     Ingredients : Dried Dragon Peppers, Honey, Yeast, and a touch of centennial hops, coriander, and cinnamon

o     Color:  Orange with a full frothy white head (Think "tang" with a head like a cappuccino)

o     Scent:  Sharp and hot with a hint of honey and cinnamon

o     Taste:  The first sip will make a dragon's eyes water, but the aftertaste of honey has a mild cooling effect.

o     Immediate Effect on Dwarves:  Dwarves report being more alert after drinking a single mead (Advantage on perception checks (active and passive) for 30 minutes, or until the second mead, then straight roll.  The third mead brings a disadvantage on perception checks (active and passive) for 3 hours.)

o     Lasting Effect of Dwarves:   Unwarned thunderous belching upon waking (disadvantage on stealth checks for the 1st hour of wakefulness after drinking even a single mead)

o     Effect on non-Dwarves:  Other races report being more alert after drinking a single mead (same as dwarves) and the need for a glass of milk!

o     Lasting Effect on non-Dwarves:  Eye watering flatulence for the 1st hour upon waking (disadvantage on stealth checks for the first hour and opponents have advantage when tracking by scent (although they may become sickened)

 

·       Axegrinder Stout

o     Ingredients:   Centenial Hops, Dwarven Barley and mushrooms, Molasses, yeast, black root extract (just a smudge for flavor)

o     Color:  Dark and creamy with a light tan, thin head (Think Guinness extra stout)

o     Scent: Earthy and rich

o     Taste: Smokey and earthy, filling

o     Immediate Effect on Dwarves: Dwarves have reported boisterous and raucous revelry after only two mugs (advantage on perform checks related to dancing and tale spinning)

o     Lasting Effects on Dwarves:  Crystal clear clarity in regards to last night’s stupidity.  (Guilt and a feeling of emasculation for doing the "Macarena" on the bar in a crowded tavern the night before.

o     Effect on non-Dwarves:   Non Dwarves are reported to feel more attractive to their preferred gender, and feel they excellent dancers. (Advantage on performance/deception/persuasion checks when dealing with romantic efforts for the first 30 minutes.  Afterwards, disadvantage on these checks until the effects wear off)

o     Lasting Effect on non-Dwarves:  Crystal clear clarity of successful (whether successful or not) romantic efforts the night before (Feeling that that barmaid was into him/her even though the individual woke up in a puddle of vomit in the alley).
