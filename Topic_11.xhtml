Game 03

Game Day

Game Date: 10/04/15

Date in Play: (4) Tarsakh 22, 1491

TLDR

  • Introducing Rowena . She has been captured by the Lord of Lance Rock .
  • Wyer woke to find Haeleeya there. Wyer figured out this is an illusion so he cast sacred flame and lit her on fire.
  • Josephus has a dream about the skull peeling flesh form his skin. Then the skull starts prepping a meal with it.
  • This is where Armond meets his patron. They make a deal. He gives them the power they need to defeat the necromancer Armond .
  • He wakes everyone up and they fight.
  • They made their way thought the cave.
  • In the main room they found Rowena tied to a plank.
  • They fought their way to the backroom and defeated the Lord of Lance Rock .

Detailed Entry

Rowena has been captured by the Lord of Lance Rock . She is tied up in his workshop and the Necromancer has been experimenting with his spells. Rowena 's friends are dead, and she is the last one left. She just watched her dwarf friend die right in front of her with a stab to the heart.

 

There may be hope. She can hear in the caves other heroes. The Necromancer sends out his patrol to tell him what is going on. He turns to Rowena and stabs her in the stomach, luckily missing anything vital.

 

Wyer sees someone coming into the circle. "Wake up!" he yells. "get up. Someone's coming." He starts gathering his things , but notices that no one is waking up.

 

The person in the circle is a girl. Wyer thinks he knows her. He goes to kick his comrades, but they are not there.

 

" Wyer . Wyer . What are you doing here?" It was Haeleeya from Red Larch . She was standing in the middle of the circle. "Come here Wyer ."

 

Wyer thought about his options and threw a Sacred Flame at her. Wyer knew this was a dream or an illusion. He ignited her. Her body completely caught on fire.

 

" Wyer , why? Why would you do this to me." she says as she falls to her knees. " Wyer what did I do for this?" she is now a skeleton still speaking. " Wyer , help me. Please."

 

The scene changed and Wyer was tied up to a stake in Red Larch . He was hearing the town constable, Harburk talking to the crowds about killing one of their own.

 

Josephus awakes to find himself back at the tree. The skull pulls itself from the tree and begins attacking him. Josephus tries to stop it, but it eventually meets flesh and starts pulling chunks off of him. It keeps going till it eventually has enough to make a meal.

 

It starts to prepare the food. Josephus watches as it starts cooking like how he would do if he were back in a kitchen. Josephus is looking down at the meat on the table. "Hurry up cook. You have men to feed." Josephus starts preparing dinner plates using chunks of his flesh to top them off. A very familiar looking girl preps the plate and places a flower on the side. She smiles and walks out the door.

***EDIT***

 

Armond was standing in the cave looking down on the bodies of his companions and himself. He is standing there in a dream like state. Surrounding his friends he sees minions of the undead quietly surrounding them ready to strike. The scene is frozen. Armond can see dust in the air standing still.

 

"Who goes to sleep in a cave with a necromancer?" a familiar voice says to Armond . Armond turns around. It is an old friend, Fenner.

 

"How? How could this be? How are you visiting me here?" Armond asks.

 

"Relax, I am not your friend you once knew. I thought you would rather deal with a familiar face. You know who I am. You have seen me before, but wearing a different mask. You have denied me many times and I thought I would give you one last chance." The man that looks like Fenner says.

 

"What do you want from me?" Armond asks realizing the choice is join the devil or die.

 

The man that looks like Fenner gets excited. His face changes shape momentarily and forms back to Finer. "It is the same deal I offered you before. From time to time I will ask a favor from you. You have permission to deny a favor, but you will only be allowed to deny 2 in a row. The 3rd favor you will not be allowed to deny. Once you accept the favor you must complete it. Upon completing a favor the rule of 3 starts over. If you fail me I will take a piece of your soul. This will be painful and you will only be able to survive so many of these painful exercises. After you pass your soul is mine to do as I wish. Your death will not be awful, there could be worst accommodations. Do you have any questions?"

 

"These sound reasonable but we will see about you taking my soul once I die." Armond replies.

 

"I wouldn't expect any less of you Armond ." The devil states with a smirk. "So you agree?"

 

"How will you help us out of this situation?" Armond questions before agreeing.

 

"I will help you by helping yourselves. I will just give you a fair chance. Here is what I can do. I can wake you up from this sleep spell, I will give you a full nights rest, I can make you feel fully energized, and (this part is my favorite) I will restore your clerics abilities so he can cast Turn Undead. The Monsters in this cave will not stand a chance." The Devil answers "So, do you agree to these terms?"

 

"I do." Armond answers.

 

"Excellent!" The devil says, clearly elated. His face jumps through a few forms.

 

"What shall I call you?" Armond questions.

 

"I go by many names, but for now you can call me your patron. Once you earn it I will give you a new name to call me." the Devil answers. "I am going to call on my first favor now. In this cave there is a necromancer. He used to answer to me. He has denied me, left me, and hid from me. He moved on to a new power, a power I can't see or feel. It is foreign to me. I don't like it. I want this man destroyed and I want it down by your weapon. Before he dies tell him I said hello."

 

"This favor will be done for you post haste." Armond answers.

 

"Then it is time I wake you up are you ready?" Armond Patron motions to his body. Armond is then pulled into his body and feels energized.

 

On waking up he says "You don't disappoint." but this was drowned out by Wyers booming voice.

 

***END EDIT***

 

Wyer hears a voice in his head "It is time to wake up and start turning." Wyer did exactly as he was told. He work up and Casted Turn undead. Good thing too, they were surrounded. All but 5 zombies and skeletons turned and started to run. The party didn't seem to let them get far. All but three were killed.

 

The Lord of Lance Rock could be heard talking to one of the skeletons when it returned. "My precious did you kill them all for daddy?... What do you mean they woke up? I hit them solid with a sleep spell. There is no way they work up… what do you mean they forced you to run?... They killed all but you three? FINE! I will just have to do it myself." The heroes hear a sound of a crashing skeleton against the wall.

 

The Lord of Lance Rock goes back to his workshop and starts talking to Rowena "More friends of yours come to try and save you?"

 

Rowena "Which friends? The ones you turned into your playthings?"

 

"It is no matter." he said "I will deal with them like I did the others."

  

The Party decided to split up. The Monk, Barikhol , charges in while the others discover a chest room. The place is trapped. There is a bolder set to collapse on top of the heroes. Josephus jumps further into the room while Armond jumps back out of it. The rock doesn't fall.

 

There is a peephole that Josephus can use to shoot arrows from. The rest of the party ran the other way.

 

Barikhol charged in to attack. Crawling hands and zombies started charging after him. He got a good hit on the necromancer, but then the skeletons jumped in.

 

Josephus took a shot from the window and missed the necromancer, but the arrow landed on the plank so Rowena could use it. She starts rubbing the ropes against the arrow tip trying to get out. The others run in and are meet with crawling hands and a large cloaked zombie.

 

Barikhol ducks out of the way of an arrow the skeleton releases. It hits another spot right next to Rowena . She calls out "Seriously guys I was much safer before you arrived!"

 

Rowena escapes and tries to help Barikhol . The skeletons down him and surround Rowena . The necromancer escapes. The rest of the party keeps fighting and are slowly losing. This is when Josephus decides he is doing no good from the peephole position.

 

He makes a mad dash to the room getting there in the nick of time to join the fight. The rest of the party work together fighting their way to Barikhol .

 

Once he was healed and they all started working together they were finally able to defeat the room.

 

Rowena took a moment to find a sheet to cover up her friend. Barikhol stops to assist her and they realize they know one another. They make their way to the rest of the group to hunt down the necromancer.

 

Once they step into the room they see in the middle of the room a pedestal fashioned of severed arms arranged to hold a glowing crystal box. Above the box is a dark sigil with and eyelike slit. Looking around the room they don't see the necromancer.

 

Wyer starts to destroy the book when Rowena stops him. "Wait. This box may help us." At the time Rowena couldn't remember exactly why.

 

When they started talking about the box the Necromancer steps out from behind a tapestry saying, "isn't it beautiful. Don't you want to embrace it? Don't you fear it?" This is when everyone jumped him.

 

The fight didn't last too long, but at one point he did take down Barikhol and syphon all his health. The same trick didn't work for Josephus and it was finally Armond that ran him through with the Glaive.

 

***EDIT***

 

Armond whispers, "A mutual friend says hello."

 

The necromancer looks over at Armond with realization and tries to say "how?" but it was lost in his death.

 

***END EDIT***

 

In the room they found a few things . There was coin. They also found a sphere and a wand. Wyer decided the box was clearly evil and he destroyed it with fire. He brought it along.

 

The party started gathering up all the dead, placing the friends of Rowena into a separate group. Wyer asked her for information so he can properly put them to rest.

 

The heroes defeated the Lord of Lance Rock . Where will they go next? What was that Dark sigil? Will all the weather return to normal now that the evil has been removed?
