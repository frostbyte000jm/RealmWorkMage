Game 11

Game Day

Game Date: 11/07/16

In Play Date:: (5) Mirtul 09, 1491; (10) Marpenoth 3, 1491

TLDR

  • Tyelknowle didn’t return to the camp like he told Josephus he would. Rowena went looking for him.
  • Rowena came running back quickly. She was being chased by some kind of Troll monster.
  • They fought and killed the Beast. The Beast was confused why Rowena, an “earth Mage” was attacking him.
  • The Heroes tried to trick the guards, but that didn’t work. Barikhol broke open the side door. They opened the first door to their left and it turned out to be a Monk training room.
  • Inside a Monk named Hellenrae. The heroes and she fought. She took the heroes down one by one and after each hero would fall, she gave the option for the rest of the heroes to leave. They did not and she eventually captured them all.
  • Josephus didn’t survive the fight.
  • Tyelknowle was captured by the beast from the beginning. He finally got free, but too late to help his friends. Ty picked up his things and went searching for the Mud-Bloods.
  • In the end he found them, five months later.

Detailed Entry

/Cut Scene\

 

Tyelknowle told Josephus , "I will meet you back at camp. I am going to watch the other side and hopefully see some activity inside. This place is a lot quieter than I thought."

 

Ty found an advantage point where he could see through the glass roof into a garden and could see into the rear side of the monastery. While he was watching Ty heard a noise that came flying out of the bushes. In a blink of the eye Ty had his dagger out and ready to throw.

 

The beast turned out to be a rabbit. It would actually have made a good food for later. Just before Ty threw the knife the area got really dark. When he turned around he got blindsided by the handle of a sword.

 

Tyelknole woke to sunlight hitting his face. He was locked in a cage and could hear fighting off in the distance. From the magic burst and battle yells he could tell were his friends. Ty inspected the cage. It was made out of sturdy wood and the door was tied shut in a thick complicated knot. The rope was heavy and difficult to manipulate. Ty could see his equipment just out of reach of the cage.

 

Using his clothes he took them off and tied them in a knot. He flung them out to try and wrap around his pack. Ty was nervous the beast would return, so between each throw he would listen. He finally got his shirt to pull his knife close enough to where he could reach his foot out and grab it.

 

Ty started cutting the rope.

 

/End Cut Scene\

 

***Earlier***

Everyone returned to the camp, everyone, that is, except Ty. Josephus said "He will be back soon. We should wait for him." After some time passed the party started to worry that he was not going to return.

 

Rowena went scouting for Ty. Josephus tried watching her from a vantage point, but eventually lost her to the darkness. Rowena found some tracks that lead her to a good lookout point that can see into the monastery. She peered over a rock to take a look.

 

Rowena felt her ear twitch. She had the feeling she was no longer alone. She turned around and saw this monstrous beast bring down a sword onto her. She dived out of the way and ran. After some close encounters with the beast almost catching her Rowena finally made it back to the camp where the others were alerted to her coming with company, by Josephus .

 

The beast was strong and could do some powerful damage. During the fight Rowena tried to grab it with "Max's Earthen Grasp". The creature got confused and said "Earth Mage, why are you attacking me?" Eventually, Armond cast a spell that covered himself in ice shards and taunted the beast. The beast took two attacks on him and the ice shards shot through the beast and put it down.

 

The beast was laying on the ground trying to breathe, but having difficulty. Rowena walked up to it and it asked, "Earth Mage, did I not serve the Cult of the Black Earth well?

 

Rowena said, "No, you did good. You can rest in peace now."

 

The beast said, "I… don't… understand." and he went to sleep. The beast started to disintegrate till he was nothing but a pile of sand on the ground.

 

 

The heroes took a rest and when Ty didn't return in the night, they feared the worst and came up with a plan to get into the monastery.

 

The choice was to attack the monastery or take guards clothes and pose as them to get into the monastery.

 

Rowena played the part as the damsel in distress. She was pretending that she broke her ankle. She got the guards attention, but as they started to approach, something showed from Rowena that something wasn't right and the guards looked at each other, then started to run to the building.

 

Rowena yelled "Wait!" and sounded convincing enough the guards paused.

 

She started stumbling up to them when one of the guards asked "Wasn't it your other leg?"

 

Rowena replied, "ummmm…"

 

They ran towards the front doors: Josephus shot an arrow. Barikhol threw his throwing axe. Armond blasted at them, but missed. Rowena casted sleep and took down one of the one of the guards. The other guard kept running and the doors opened up when he ran up.

 

The heroes took the fallen guard and ran off.

 

They found a place in the woods, stripped him, tied him up, and splashed water on him to wake him up. They questioned him about how many people were in the monastery and if they have taken a half elf as a prisoner. The guard wouldn't speak, but Armond can be very convincing with a knife into the leg. After a bit of screaming he admitted there were at least 50 inside. He tried to say that he doesn't know of any half elf, but Josephus felt there was deceit behind his words.

 

They tried to get more out of him, but the guard shutdown. He told Armond , "kill me if you must, but I am not saying another word."

 

Armond replied with, "ok." And then he slit the guard’s throat.

 

They decided to break into the Monastery. Barikhol and Rowena snuck up to the side door. Barikhol kicked open the door. Right after the sounds from the room to the left got really quiet.

 

Barikhol walks up to the door and opens it. Inside was a dojo. There was a female tiefling and 4 human students. She says to the group of monks facing the intruders "Class, our guest are here to learn how to dance, let's show them what you have learned."

 

The fight began. Josephus and Barikhol jumped into the room and started taking down the monks. Armond ran outside to see a spot through the window so he can misty-step in. Rowena ran around the other side to go in through another door to attack the monks from behind.

 

Josephus and Barikhol knocked out one of the fighters, Rowena casted sleep to drop the rest, the last person standing was the Tiefling. She stood her ground ready for the heroes. The moment one of them moved she darted lightning quick dropping Barikhol . Josephus swung his sword at her and she blocked it, catching it by clasping her hands on the flat of the blade. Rowena casted "Max's Earthen Grasp". The Monk punched her way out of the earthly hand.

 

"I train with earth mages, like you, daily". The monk said. She took two quick jabs at Josephus and kicked him in the head knocking him out. Armond blasted a black and red bolt, which slammed into the tiefling knocking her against the wall. Rowena sent fire blast flames from her back, which burst in a set of 3 on the tiefling monk.

 

The tiefling monk kicked off the wall and landed in front of Rowena . After a combo of blows Rowena fell to the floor.

 

On the ground Josephus opened up his eye and was sliding something out of his pocket. He popped open a bottle of healing potion and took a drink. He popped open a second bottle and gave it to Barikhol . Armond blasted the tiefling from behind sending her out into the hall way.

 

She hollers back, "I am giving you a choice. You can leave, or I can come back in."

 

Barikhol questioned, "What do you mean come back in?"

 

In reply the Tiefling jumped over Rowena back into the room. She took up a fighting stance.

 

Barikhol walked over to her and bowed. In reply the Tiefling went to kick him in the head. She missed. Barikhol said "we will take our friend and leave."

 

"No, she is mine." the Tiefling replied.

 

Josephus said, "Poo Poo on this." (It was a great line, I know Josephus used it somewhere else, but it felt better here) and jumped in to attack the tiefling. He took a couple of blows, a kick to the throat, and went down.

 

The tiefling said. "I will give you this choice again. These two are forfeit."

 

Barikhol and Armond looked at each other. After a moments look Armond said, "We have come this far…" Then blasted at the Tiefling.

 

"Fools." She said as she deflected Barikhols strikes. She then did a combo of blows to him taking him out of the fight.

 

"Last chance." The tiefling said. She was winded. She was bleeding. She was hurt. "Your friends are mine. Leave!"

 

"Not a chance." Armond answered. The tiefling charged him. He swung his glaive and she flipped over it kicking him, and following up with a double punch.

 

Armond fell to the floor.

 

//Cut scene\\

Soldiers come running into the room. Two run up to the tiefling, "Master Hellenrae , are you hurt?"

 

She waves them off and points the intruders. "Check them, strip them, and lock them up. These are the heroes of Red Larch that defeated Larrakh . I want to get to know them… intimately."

 

Tyelknowle stands outside on a hill looking into the window. He was too late. He watched the party get dragged out of the room and away. "There are too many of them. No way am I able to take them on. Tymora what should I do?"

 

Ty was still getting his gear back together. The Monster went through all his stuff. Ty reaches into his pouch and pulls out a slip of paper. It is a bank deposit receipt. Something Ty has kept on him for many years. He remembers the day this came into his possession.

 

He was on the streets of Phsant in the country of Thesk. He “acquired” this from a noble man. This was one of his days when he was with the Mud-Bloods .

 

Ty packs his things and goes. Over the next weeks Ty is seen in different cities speaking to people in Thieves Cant, slipping coins to people, or handing over a bag of goods.

 

***Later***

Drums playing in a clearing under the canopy of the High Forest. A caravan of travelers seem to have made a permanent structure in the forest. This is their home or at least one of their homes. On a sign near the entrance to this clearing reads, "You are now entering the home of the Mud-Bloods . All who enter here are responsible for themselves as well as their company."

 

The clearing is a very small town, built for maybe up to 300 people. Their homes are built up in the trees, structures designed by wood-elfs. They have small structures along the road that sells goods (may or may not have been acquired via… donation), structures for place to get a good meal, structures that could work as an inn, and more. Tonight it seems all the towns people have made their way to a very large clearing where they keep their caravans and some of their livestock. They are having a gathering celebrating what seems to be a coming of age for 3 young men.

 

Sitting on a large chair on a platform is this blue Tiefling female. She is smiling and enjoying the festivities. She looks over at the crowd and her eyes fall on a particular person. Her expression of joy turned into one of shock, like she has seen a ghost.

 

Tyelknowle comes walking up followed by a stone dwarf. " Joclyn , I have a favor to ask of you."
