Detailed Entry

The adventure starts on the same day we left off with, Mirtul 02, 1491. The Heroes are talking about what to do next. It was eventually decided to investigate the town to find the members of The Believers . Before they got started they went to the bathhouse to wash up. This didn't take too long and the heroes were ready.

 

Tyelknowle thought the best place for him to be was a lookout. He took a long way around and propped himself on top of a house. He stayed in a corner and laid himself flat. Unless someone was actively looking for him, he had a very low chance of being spotted.

 

Armond took Barikhol and Rowena with him to see Harburk . Harburk wasn't a fan of the adventurers harassing and roughing up his towns people. He asked them "Have you ever done anything like this?" Armond answered that he had. Harburk amended "Have you done this, not as a soldier?" Harburk knew that Armond was a soldier and part of being a soldier was to get information for people. Soldiers had their ways, but it is quite different when you’re a servant of the citizens. Towns, cities, and kingdoms have fallen apart because a ruler has treated her/his citizens like they would on the battle fields. Harburk knew of this all too well and didn’t want that kind of behavior here.

 

Harburk made the heroes a deal. He knew that he couldn't objectively look at this, but he couldn't allow these adventures going around town unmonitored. So the deal was said, "I will allow you to do your investigation, but you will not rough up any of my citizens. I will accompany you to make sure you follow my deal. It will be your investigation and I will not interfere, unless I feel my citizens are in danger."

 

Armond replied "You have my word, I will not harm your citizens, but I will defend myself if needed. I will also have a talk with our friend Josephus to make sure he doesn't get out of hand."

 

They left to go get Josephus and Rowena stayed behind to signal Ty. Ty was busy watching something else happening. A human male about the age of 500… no wait, that can't be right, well he was older… Anyway, he was escorting a young boy out of the cellar in Waelvurs Wagonworks. They were heading out towards the back gate.

 

Ty looked down to see Rowena and signaled her to go around the other way. She did and Ty ran after the man and the boy. Ty saw the two head south to skirt around the trees and stay away from the main road. Ty knew that they would be out of Rowena 's sight so he left Bert (pronounced Bael'Ru) to let Rowena know where he is going.

 

Rowena came around the corner and noticed the gate was used, but wasn't closed all the way. She continued walked around hoping to find something, but found nothing. It wasn't till a mouse came running up to her and started running towards the east road that she knew where to go. Rowena has seen weirder things and knows that many animals can be trusted. Well the cute ones anyway. After she followed Bert to the road she saw Ty so Rowena scooped down to pick up Bert. Bert settled in nicely on her shoulder as if this was second nature and she jogged lightly towards him.

 

Ty was keeping a good distance between the man and the boy, but close enough so they couldn't try and lose him. He finally watched them head east on Cairn Road and go into a house. Ty waited for Rowena and Bert. Once they were together Ty filled Rowena in on what was happening and she ran back to inform the others while Ty stayed watch.

 

Armond , Barikhol , and Josephus got back to Harburk looking over the hole and the workers trying to barricade it off and map it so they can create a plan to fix it. When they got back they decided to first go and see Ilmeth , the owner of Wagonworks. They decided to go without the elves since they were not back, but the moment they reached this decision Rowena returned and filled in the rest of the party on what Tyelknowle was doing. They continued to Wagonworks.

 

Ilmeth wasn't there. But they got to talk to Stannor . Stannor , the Halfling, is known as the town drunk, Harburk explained. Stannor seemed really nervous talking to the adventures. They started asking him questions about the cellar. Stannor told them that he once walked into the cellar and followed the path all the way down to the door. When he returned Ilmeth told him that if he ever mentioned this that he would kill him. Stannor mentioned that Ilmeth and others sometimes go down there. Stannor didn't know where to find Ilmeth , he guessed his home.

 

The adventures left and headed over to Ilmeths home and Rowena ran down the road to inform Ty what is going on. Ty said he would stay here to watch the two that entered the house. Rowena returned and to find that Armond wants to explore the cave again instead.

 

Armond was worried that someone would blow up the cave, or hide the evidence, or worse. It is better they get down there before any of that happens. Harburk was in agreement with this plan as he wanted to see what was in the cave.

 

They got down there and entered into the Main door. The statues were moved to open up the center hallway. The Heroes went down and peered inside the room. This room they didn't enter before and they didn't stay in either. There was a man sitting in the corner. He was an older gentleman and he just waited to be found. Rowena stayed in the room with him while the others continued to the rest of the cave.

 

Rowena started a conversation with the man. His name is Baragustas he is an old member of The Believers . He talked about The Believers and how he and Aerego were two of the original members. He explained how the Delvers would warn the town when bad things were coming. Rowena read the inscription on the large stone in the middle of the room. "Displease not the Delvers" written in common.

 

Rowena asked about the earth mage. Baragustas told her, "I believe Larrakh is a spirit of the Delvers here to help us believers interpret the stones."

 

The other heroes were in the large room where they battled Larrakh (Yeah, I decided to keep his name. It may be a little funny, but it is his name. He should own it.) just hours ago. Armond went over to the far corner of the room to see if he could find a secret door, but saw nothing. Harburk went to look at one of the Delvers. He was amazed this was beneath Red Larch this entire time and he never knew. Harburk saw the bodies of the Bringers of Woe and the Goblins. He recognized one of the bringers. It was one of the workers at the quarry.

 

On their way out of the room, Josephus tripped and kicked a rock. Once the rock struck ground everything, but the delvers started to float. After swimming their way out of the room, Barikhol had an idea. He took a rock and slammed it on the ground. Everything dropped and made a loud rumble. Rowena came running out to make sure they were ok. Barikhol did it again to demonstrate.

 

When the crash happened a second time, Josephus heard something come from the north. It sounded like people running off. Josephus alerted everyone to this and they ran up there. Rowena running back to Baragustas . Once the heroes arrived in the room with the bodies they found Grund the half-orc, the Orc-Fingers (Pickle), vendor. He fell to the ground crying. "I'm sorry, please don't hurt me."

 

Josephus , Armond , and Barikhol started intimidating him to get him to talk. This made the half orc cry harder. They finally got from him that Dornen and Ilmeth ran. Armond ran back to get Baragustas and Rowena . Once they got to the surface. Ilmeth and Dornen were gone. Harburk took Baragustas and Grund to the station.

 

Rowena ran off to get Tyelknowle . Ty snuck up to the house to make sure the man and the child were still inside. He could see the man was cleaning up the house like he was expecting a visitor. Rowena waited for Ty to spot her. He came out of the tree. She caught him up on what was happening and Ty decided to come with her. He doesn't think the man and the boy are going anywhere.

 

They got back and the party decided to pay Ilmeth 's house a visit. When no one was home Ty opened up the door so they could look around. After searching the house, Ty found Ilmeth 's robes and metal mask and Rowena found Ilmeth 's journal for The Believers .

 

They decided to head on over to the father and sons house. Harburk explained that their names are Rotharr Hatherhand and his son Braelen . They came up to the house and knocked on the door. Josephus made his way to the back door said "We have you surrounded!"

 

Ty replied "No we don't." Armond and Barikhol shrugged.

 

Rotharr open the door. "How may I help you?"

 

The heroes held up the robes and said "We come to ask you about this." Rotharr tried to close the door and the heroes made sure that didn't happen.

 

Rotharr said "Look, I am not supposed to be talking about this."

 

Ty replied, "People are dead and I saw you and your son sneaking down into the caves to cover it up."

 

Rotharr realizing how all this looks quickly said "wait. No. That is not what happened." after a moments decision Rotharr invited them in.

 

They took a seat and Josephus made his way in. Rotharr explained that he didn't take his son down into the cave, but to go get his son out of the caves. His son came into the room about this time and Rotharr brought him over to sit on his lap. "There is a ritual that The Believers do when initiating into the group. My son was in the middle of this ritual and when all this happened, I was worried about my son and I was also worried about people finding out about The Believers . So I ran down there to get him. He was in the middle of his fasting. Baragustas was down there to keep watch on him as the ritual allows, but he stayed behind so my son and I could get to safety."

 

On further questioning Rotharr didn't know anything about dead bodies. He knew Albaeri and Dornen but no one else of The Believers . Rotharr was expecting Albaeri to come over, and that is why he was straightening up. She would help inform him and his son on what to do now that the ritual was botched.

 

Ty told the heroes "I believe them." and then he told Rotharr and Braelen to say here. He also ask Harburk who could they trust if they needed to run. Harburk still surprised by how big this believers conspiracy was thought about it and said Kaylessa .

 

They left here and headed over to Dornen's place. After Dornen answered the door they pushed their way in. Dornen asked Harburk , "Are you going to just let them do this?"

 

Harburk said "Yes, I am." You could see that Harburk was enraged over this situation.

 

//Cut Scene\\

Harburk lived in this peaceful town all his life and had never had to deal with this kind of thing from his people. Unknown to the heroes, Harburk was once invited to be a part of The Believers . He meet with Dornen and Dornen told him about the community of people who help one another. They get together in secret and share secrets and more. Harburk asked Dornen if what they did was legal. Dornen, who was a friend to Harburk explained they are not hurting anyone. Harburk pieced together they probably do some trading and maybe some gambling. Harburk appreciated the offer, but eventually declined so he was not ever accidently caught up in this. He told Dornen, I will keep a blind eye to your group as long as you don't go hurting anyone. I don't want any mobs in this town. Dornen, knowing Harburk 's past told him that he would never let it become something like that.

//End Cut Scene\\

 

The heroes started questioning Dornen when they heard something in the back room. It was Ilmeth , hiding in Dornen's room with a sword. He was nervous and Ty could actively see the blade shaking. Ty said to him "You have no idea how much I want you to try and hit me with that thing." Ilmeth dropped it.

 

Josephus demanded from them "I know you brought the bodies back here, where are they!"

 

Ilmeth replied, "We’re not stupid enough to bring the bodies back here…" and realizing that he just admitted to the bodies started to turn red. Dorned gave him a look that you give idiots when they just did something more stupid than normal.

 

They started asking who all is in The Believers . They intimidated it out of them to give them a list. Dornen said he would. They then asked who all was involved in the killings. After some pressure they admitted that Albaeri was the only other one that knew. Ilmeth also informed the heroes that Grund was tricked into helping, "We lied to him to make him think you guys were the bad guys and the bodies needed to be hidden to help prove it. Grund isn't smart, so he believed us."

 

Armond wanted to know where Larrakh was. They didn't know. Armond didn't believe them. Armond snapped Ilmeth 's leg. Ilmeth laid on the ground writhing and yelling in pain.

 

Harburk stepped up and said "We had a deal."

 

Armond argued, "They killed people."

 

Harburk said, "This is not how it works."

 

Armond shrugged him off and went to attack them again when Tyelknole stepped in.

 

Ty said "Stop this. They are cooperating. They are telling us what they know. I understand you have a vendetta against the earth cult and we will find them. But think about what you're doing. This is not helping." Armond backed off. Ty looked over at Harburk who had his hand on his sword, and he nodded to Ty as he let go. Ty reached down and healed Ilmeth ’s leg.

 

They took Dornen and Ilmeth back to the station and released Grund and Baragustas . Harburk asked Baragustas to not leave town and asked Grund to help him bring in Albaeri.

 

Harburk told Armond that he should meet with Sheppard Larmon Greenboot. He mentioned to Harburk this morning that he saw something and Harburk suggested to Larmon to seek your group out. He will probably be getting dinner at the tavern in the next couple hours.

 

The heroes started to go back to the tavern and Harburk pulled Armond off to the side.

 

"You and I had a deal. I thought I could trust you enough with this. I thought what I asked was fair." Harburk started.

 

Armond "They killed people. They are helping a Black Earth Cultist. They deserve worse…"

 

Harburk cut Armond off "It doesn't matter, you and I agreed that you would not rough up any of my citizens. You are not Judge and Executioner. Now it is out of respect for you and what you have done for this town that I let this one go…"

 

Armond interrupted "It is out of respect for you that I don't burn this city to the ground."

 

Harburk gets that knowing look in his eye. "There it is. That evil brewing inside you. You need to get control of it before it controls you."

 

"You don't want to get in my way." Armond begun

 

"No, but I will. I have seen people like you. Son, I have seen way more battles than you could imagine. I forgive once, understand?" Harburk warned.

 

Tyelknowle said "We get it, you both have large penises and they shoot very far. Let's move on."

 

Harburk understood what Ty was doing and nodded, "I forgive once, that is all" and he walked away.

 

At the tavern everyone was sitting around having drinks talking about what happened and where to go next. Armond mentioned doing a favor for Kaylessa . A friend of hers, Brother Eardon , said that a delegation he knew was missing. At first they thought the bodies found in the caves were the delegation, but the age doesn't make sense. The bodies are about 3 tendays old, when the delegation was leaving two tendays ago.

 

The rest of the party agreed to help Armond out with this. This is when Larmon Greenboot came walking in. He was even carrying a shepherd’s stick. He looked around the room and walked up to the group. " Harburk said I should talk to you about what I found. When I was out yesterday I saw four unmarked graves. These were not shallow graves, but looked like the person took some time to do this correctly."

 

He continued, "When I saw Harburk I asked him who passed away. He didn't know anything. The graves are not exactly close to here, but this is the only town close enough were I thought someone would know something. The graves also looked relatively new, so I figured it had to be recent. If you want I can take you out there in the morning."

The heroes continued discussing this when all a sudden Rowena started to glow. She felt pressure that magic wants to release. When she let it go she could feel something different. It wasn't normal to her. The spell that was casted was dancing lights and they were illuminating above everyone's head. When asked about this Rowena said "I don't know what happened. I got to go research." she stood up and ran out. Her body started to release a spell again. She pushed through the doors and left.

 

Rowena was standing in a familiar place. A place she hasn't been to in years. She is standing in the library at Candle Keep. Rowena knows this library is hundreds of miles south of Red Larch . She doesn't know how she got here. She decided to take advantage of this and do some research.
