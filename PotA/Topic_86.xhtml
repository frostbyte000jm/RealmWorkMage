Finding Ourselves

Overview

Theme or Threat: Theme

Type: Soft

Barikhol, Armond, and Rowena have recently been captured and tortured for over 5 months. They all have histories that many adventures do. Most adventurers do not take the call under good times. For many something bad has to happen. They are lucky if it is just one thing.

 

In this Theme we will be exploring these three heroes journey back to finding their center. They will be tempted to veer off course, but hopefully, together, they can find the strength to raise themselves back up to be the heroes that the citizens of Red Larch know them to be.

 

This theme also encompases Tyelknowle and Grimble.

Tyelknowle is struggling with returning to the Mud-Blood family or continuing his quest with his fellow Heroes of Red Larch.

Grimble is still deciding if working with these adventurers will help him with his goals.
