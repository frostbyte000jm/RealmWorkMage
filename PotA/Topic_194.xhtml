Detailed Entry

Our story begins with the heroes of Red Larch huddled inside of the church on the Cult of the Crashing Waves Hideout in the Riverguard Keep .

 

The Keep is under the disguise of being sworn to the Oder of the Gauntlet by flying their flag for all to see.

 

The heroes are with two female sailors Teria and Sesa . They all discuss a plan. Barikhol and Teria will sneak out and do some general reckoning and will report back what they found.

 

At that moment they all heard a roar come from the west. "I think I will start by looking east." Barikhol joked.

 

They headed out into the keep sticking to the shadows, but having the moonlight to help them see, to recon the area. They found the Barracks, they found the southern wall and tower, they saw the chain that is keeping the boats in the bay is connected to that tower, they see there are not many out in the keep, and other than the sound that came from the main hall to the west it was pretty quiet.

 

Barikhol wanted to get a closer look into the bay. He placed his dwarvin foot on the stairs and a river monster started pulling himself out and crawl towards the stairs. When Barikhol backed up it dove back into the water.

 

Teria went down to the dock to check out her boat. Barikhol followed behind her. Teria stepped onto the plank and it made a creek noise.

 

Barikhol , being seasoned for this sort of thing rolled off the edge and clung onto the side of the boat. A water genasi suck his head up. Teria knew exactly who this was. " Shoalar , I thought I would find you here."

 

" Teria , my love." Shoalar said upon seeing her.

 

"You took my boat, and you have the nerve to call me 'your love'?" Teria spat back at Shoalar .

 

Next to Shoalar was a Halfling. " Shoalar , who is this?"

 

Shoalar stood there with his arms crossed and a grin crept onto his face. " Pike , this is Teria . I told you about her." Pike gained a knowing look in his face.

 

Teria walked onto her boat (well, it was kind of his right now, but soon to be her boat) and climbed up to where Shoalar and Pike stood. She drew her sword and pointed it right at Shoalar and said "Give me back my boat."

 

Pike drew his battle staves but Shoalar just stood there arms crossed chuckling. "What is your plan here sweetheart? You can't take the boat anywhere. This won't end well for you."

 

Barikhol knew exactly what this guy is doing. "Really, do all bad-guys read from the same playbook?" he thought to himself. Barikhol needed to sneak up to them before they stalled long enough to get whatever to happen to happen. Barikhol pulled himself up, and made his way behind Shoalar and… CREEEEEEEK… " stupid boat". Barikhol lashed out and the fight begun.

 

The four of them started fighting on the top of the cabin. Teria swung her sword and was grappled by Shoalar and placed between him and Barikhol .

 

Barikhol kicks Pike and he goes flying off the boat, but last second he managed to hang on.

 

Meanwhile back at the church, Armond tried to get everyone to get quiet. "Something's wrong."

 

At the same moment Sesa broke in, "They were caught."

 

Pirate Bandits were waking up on the boat and were making their way up to the deck.

 

"Shit!" Armond cursed and ran out the door. Armond moved his hands in a whirling gesture and fired them right at Scholar on the boat. Teria saw it coming knocked her head back to hit Scholar and moved out of the way of the Eldritch Blast.

 

Scholar went flying off the boat, missing the edge of the stone and splashed into the water.

 

Rowena also came running out and conjured her Scorching Ray in front of her. She blasted it to the boat and at the last minute made it split hitting Pike and a group of bandits.

 

Grimble starts running over clanking the whole way from the church, to the dock, and then leaps towards the bandits on the back of the boat.

 

Sesa runs the other way. She leaps up to fight the guard on the north wall. You here "hey you there." We get a shot of Sesa 's face and we see in her eyes that she notices something. A wide grin comes across her face.

 

A large funnel wave comes flying out of the ocean and turns in the air then pounds down on Barikhol and Teria .

 

Grimble lands on the boat and starts attacking the Pirate Bandits from behind. Teria , soaking wet rolled off the cabin and started assisting.

 

Pike made his way back onto the cabin and attacked Barikhol . Barikhol spun around and kicked Pike off the ship again. This time Pike 's head hit the stone before falling forward into the water. Barikhol could hear the pop squish of Pike 's head hitting the east wall.

 

Armond powered up his Eldritch Blast and launched it towards the last remaining pirates on the boat. The blast hit the boat shattering parts of the side and from the exterior walls of the cabin blasted the pirates off the side.

 

Grimble and Teria faced off against one last pirate. He was standing there wobbling his legs, looking at both of them, he let out a battle cry of "Screw this!", and he jumped off the boat.

 

The heroes heard a loud thump from the double doors of the great hall. Rowena , loving nothing more than to solve a good mystery (Should have received an inspiration point) went sneaking over to see what was coming out of the door. A part of her hoped to see a kitty.

 

 A boar of a man came stomping outside "WHAT IS GOING ON IN THE RIVER!" he looked around and saw someone "You there!" he gestured his hands. "Come here."

 

Rowena tried to mimic his gesture, but didn't get it right at all. The man quickly yelled out "GUARD! We have an intruder!"

 

"No, no no… I was invited here to be entertainment." Rowena rebutted.

 

He stomped up to her and said "Listen Princess I know you're lying…"

 

"I am glad you think of me as a princess" Rowena quipped.

 

Ignoring her he continued "… My name is Jolliver Grimjaw and I am the only one who invites people here. But you're in luck. I may be able to find ways for you to entertain me."

 

Grimjaw signaled up to the Guard and the Guard returned the signal. Then he quickly threw his hand out and grabbed Rowena by the hair and caught his fist around her fox ear.

 

"It looks to be all clear now." the guard said.

 

"Fine!" he said as he dragged Rowena back inside.

 

The rest of the party got up to Armond including Sesa in guards cloths.

 

Some people came out of the barracks "You! Over there! Identify yourself!"

 

"Identify yourself!" Grimble shouted back.

 

Sesa quickly looked over at him with the expression of "What are you doing?" She quickly gets into view and says "All Clear!" and she holds up her hands in a symbol.

 

The Cult of the Crashing Wave member returned the symbol and went back inside.

 

Armond and Sesa recounted what just happened to Rowena .

 

"Shit!" Teria says. "Well, that was the one building we didn't want to go to."

 

"She is a fellow dwarf, we got to go save her." Barikhol says.

 

"What? I still don't think I understand that." Grimble replies.

 

Inside the grand hall Grimjaw drags Rowena down the hall past what used to be a man kneeling on the floor. Most of his body has been ripped a part, but the disturbing part is it is not clear where the rest of the person went.

 

Rowena saw two guards and a female water genasi in the room. The female water genasi spoke in a disinterested voice, "Jolli, have we a second sacrifice tonight?"

 

"No Urshnora , I found this one snooping around outside." Grimjaw says as he gives Rowena a little shake of the head.

 

Rowena looks down at the table she was standing next to, she saw a few scribbles of paper there.

"wagonload near B" "40 sp trav near W"

"Trbl goes by Heroes of RL : H/m, HE/m, D/f, D/p, E/h."

 

Urshnora looks at the girl and tells Grimjaw "I guess you can throw her in with the other servants. We will find work for… Oh… Jolly, what did you do?"

 

"What?" Grimjaw ask in confusion. "What's wrong?"

 

"Those ears. They are very interesting." Urshnora says while moving some papers around on the desk. "Not too many people have Fox ears. Dammit Grimjaw , you lead the heroes of Red Larch right to our door."

 

At this second the main hall doors open and in comes Teria .

 

Rowena used her quick thinking to zap Grimjaw by grabbing his arm and releasing built up electric energies. She looked up to see Armond gathering energy to blast Grimjaw . Rowena dove out of the way seeing the force blast move right by her and smacking Grimjaw knocking him back over the desk.

 

That is when the room heard it. Grimjaw started making growling sounds. His skin started stretching, his nails started growing, hair started sprouting all over his body, bones started breaking, and his face started stretching into a boar’s nose. Grimjaw was a wereboar.

 

At the same time Urshnora transformed into a water serpent. She moved like a whip snapping down at Rowena as she started crawling backwards under the desk.

 

Teria ran in and started fencing with a guard. Barikhol can Grimble charged in to do the same. Together they had no troubles dispatching the two female guards.

 

Chairs started flying and the table started shifting as Urshnora snapped her watery jaws at Rowena . Rowena turn and crawled away as fast as she good. Urshnora stretched to bite her in two, but somehow fell short. Urshnora , confused, looked behind her and Rowena 's Earthen Hand was holding Urshnora from going any further.

 

The Wereboar charged in for attack. Barikhol and Grimble stood their ground against it.

 

Grimble slammed his axe down on the werebores head and as he ripped his axe out of the bone Grimble could see the cut instantly healing. "Uhhhh… ummmm… Oops?" He said "Ehhh. Let me just straighten your fur…"

 

The beast roared and started clawing and biting at the intruders in his castle.

 

Urshnora , being the tricky snake she is, slithered to the right of the beast, but this wasn't Armonds first battle.

 

Armond struck at the beast and cut him. His weapon was magical and he saw that his attack worked.

 

Urshnora thinking this is a perfect time to strike snapped towards Armond , but was stopped by having a glaive rip into her and Armond spun it around and around sending her body into a whirlpool. She spit a ball of water energy at Armond . It missed and smashed against the floor.

 

"Why is it so strong?" Grimble asked.

 

Armond yelled back, "We need silver or magic!"

 

Barikhol had an idea. He reached into his pocket and pulled out some silver pieces and clinched them between his knuckles. He punched with all his might, but the beast shook his head in time and the coins went flying.

 

Rowena also had a good idea. She started preparing her spell, "take a look at this!"

 

Urshnora struck again Armond wasn't having any of it. He batted her in the face knocking her back and preventing her approach.

 

Sesa snuck in through a hole in the Keep and came running up from behind Urshnora and started stabbing at her. Teria sees her sister doing some damage to the water serpent she ran over and landed her sword deep into the serpent’s body. Urshnora tried to transform around the blade, but found she couldn't. Her body was whole again. She reformed into the water genasi and stared at Teria confused. She dropped down dead.

 

The wereboar struck wildly swinging its tusks left and right. Its tusk found home in Barikhol . Barikhol stepped back as Armond 's blade went up under the chin of the beast as he was hearing Rowena release her spell. He twisted his head to look at the dancing lights and felt the monster go limp.

 

Armond quickly jumped into action and started tying that thing up.

 

"Careful" Rowena yelled, "Shake him and he will wake up."

 

Armond had to be careful with his knots and try to think back if he ever had to hog tie a pig before. "I may need more rope." he thought.

 

The beast came out of the daze and thrashed, writhed, and screamed. Armond knew what he needed to do. He looked around the room and waited for everyone to acknowledge what must be done. He raised his glaive and struck down on the creature’s throat. The creature convulsed and then went to sleep.

 

Armond went into the kitchen and saw two workers crying and trying to clean up the place. They were chained at their ankles. "It is ok. Your capture is dead."

 

"What? How?" the kitchen staff said "he was unkillable."

 

"The Heroes of Red Larch took care of him." He replied back. You could almost sense a little bit of pride in his reply.

 

"We are chained up, please help free us." the staff called out.

 

" Sesa " Armond said, "Find the keys see what we can do."

 

Sesa found some keys in the room and toss one set to Barikhol and nodded for him to go to the room while she goes to the kitchen.

 

Barikhol caught them and he and Teria went into the servant’s quarters. "Please don't be afraid." Barikhol said as he entered the room, "Your captures are no longer your…" Barikhol saw the three men huddled on the floor. These men were very familiar to Barikhol . He remembers a time back when he owned a tavern and these men would frequent his place before going on long journeys for work.

 

"Brewmaster? How… how did…" They started confused to see a familiar face.

 

"It's ok. We got you." Barikhol said back to them. They broke. They have been captures for a very long time.

 

Once the news spread that Grimjaw had fallen the rest of the Cult of the Crashing Wave ran. For a group to kill and unkillable monster the rest of them knew they didn't stand a chance.

 

The heroes loaded up everything. Teria and Sesa started repairing parts of the ship and checking its supplies. Armond lowered the chain preventing the boat from leaving and a few hours later, they made their way down to Ironforge.

 

Once they helped the Heroes of Red Larch load up a wagon, they set sail.

 

"Don't we need to even up?" Barikhol asked.

 

"No. Keep it." Teria said "We will see you all again. I will send someone up to trade wine for ale. Also, I will see if my father can look into Tyelknowles situation in Waterdeep."

 

They parted ways. Hopefully to see each other again one day.

 

After a few days journey the heroes made their way back up to Red Larch . They unpacked, got settled, and then decided as a group to go see how Wyer is doing out at his new training center.

 

When Wyer saw them approaching he was talking to three trainees and ordered them to take position. "These are wanted criminals of Red Larch ." Wyer looked at Armond and gave a wink, "do your duty!"

 

This reminded Armond of his days in training. This was a game that was played. The young ones would be sent off against a seasoned warrior and the warrior would stop their advance and normally get a couple laughs doing it.

 

The three trainees charged and Armond held up his glaive, slid it alongside ones practice sword, nudged him with his hip, and as he was falling to the ground caught the second ones sword hand. He turned him around and gave him a kick in the pants into the third guy and watched them fall to the ground.

 

"How did he beat you?" Wyer asked the recruits.

 

They answered with "He is too strong." "Too fast." "Come on, this is one of the mighty heroes of Red Larch !"

 

"You forget you first lesson." Wyer said "Patience. We are not the front line. We wait for the enemy to come to us, then we plan and attack. But maybe we could try running head first into action like fools some more and see how well we do?"

 

The young recruits looked a little embarrassed. "Come on, up. Three Laps." Wyer told them and then turned to give a mighty handshake and hugs to his dear friends. "It has been too long."

 

They sat around a table having a meal. The temple is in the process of being built, the recruits are out practicing and working. Wyer would have to help give guidance to a few people every now and then during lunch.

 

A man approached and Wyer looked up. "Ahhh, Leokas ! Pelor sent you right on time. There are some people I would like you to meet, but I suspect this isn't the first time you have met them."

 

Barikhol stood up and gave him a friendly handshake. "Man, memories of my tavern keep showing up lately. Friends, Leokas and I met years ago in my tavern. He helped collect a bounty on a pretty bad dude. He is a good man."

 

Leokas replies back to Barikhol "I remember you used to make a mean brew."

 

"Like this one?" and Barikhol pulls out a keg from their wagon.

 

"I remember you." Leokas said to Rowena .

 

Rowena was feeling a little self-conscious about her ears and pulled her hood up. "Have trouble with any good books lately?"

 

"No." He said with a smirk "I try to stay away from them."

 

"When Leokas mentioned that he met someone with fox ears, I knew there was only one person that could be." Wyer explained. "Friends, Leokas has been working for my guard for some time. He does a great job, and I hear you all have a tough time keeping clerics." Leokas was in mid drink and paused looking concerned "You really need to quit losing them like that. I would like to suggest working with Leokas . I think he would make a good addition to you group."

 

The heroes of Red Larch looked at each other and agreed.

 

"Now, tell me about your adventures since we last talked…" Wyer opened up to the table for story.

 

Armond started telling the tale of what they have done and what the cults have also been up to.

 

After stories and lunch the lot made their way back to Red Larch . Wyer leans over to Rowena and said "Looks like Armond has improved in his storytelling." Rowena laughed at a story almost 6 months ago when they were at the Feathergale Spire .

This is Nathan's first cut scene. You will see these from time to time. These are parts of the story your characters don't know, but happen in the world. They are usually set up for foreshadowing or sometimes just for entertainment.

 

Teria and Sesa are on their boat sometime in the next couple of weeks. They seem to be watching a crew of people working and moving big crates of their wine.

 

"Sister." Sesa started.

 

"Hummm?" Teria questioned. They are keeping their voices down for some reason.

 

"Why didn't you just tell them that you let them steal your ship?"

 

Teria quickly jerked her head around looking at her sister and then the crew. "The heroes needed a good story. If I just told them we were pirates and we were setting up the Watercult for a big score, do you think they would have come?"

 

"Some pirates we are." Sesa replied. "You gave them all the loot."

 

Teria started to laugh, but kept it in. She looked over at Sesa and then looked down between the cracks on her right.

 

Sesa had to lean her head over and try and see what she saw, and then the sun moved just right and she saw the shine of treasure. "What? How?"

 

We see next to Sesa 3 more people. It was the two in the kitchen, and the one they tied up in the church. "We were loading the ship up for almost a month before you all arrived."

 

We see the scene in clarity now. A man standing in front of them takes a long bow "Thank you for your business. Your wine will turn up a mighty profit, sweet stuff. Maybe I will run into your sweet ass one day sailing these waters." he says with an ugly smile as he turns walks the plank to his boat as they start to sail off.

 

Teria unties her hands and loosens the ropes for her sister. "ugh, male pirates." Sesa says. "They are so gross. Now what are we going to do?"

 

Teria looks down at her compass and watches the needle follow along with their boat. "We wait for them to get home, and then my sweet ass pays them a visit."
