Barikhol Collaborative Adventure

Stories

Heroes: Barikhol , Armond , Josephus

Barikhol 's father was a brewer, and they took a trip to the Great Anuroch Desert to collect one of the primary ingredients for their family specialty; Fire Mead.  About two score days after leaving Mithiril Hall, they were attacked by a band of orcs, and Barikhol 's father, was no fighter.  His father was killed orcs, and Barikhol was severely wounded.

 

Thankfully, being a hardy dwarf, Barikhol did not succomb to his wounds, and was able to survive at the edge of the Anuroch.  After some time, he went back to the scene of his father's death, and rummaged around the remains of the wagon.   Barikhol was able to get enough of the equipment together to build a small distillery, and there he sat for a year, surviving and brewing meads and ales.

 

After a time, the distillery attracted a Watcher from the White Worm Citadel, a ranger/monk.  He and Barikhol became close friends, and he took Barikhol to the citadel for training.   Barikhol has sworn vengeance on orcs in the area south of Mithiril Hall.

 

Upon returning from training, Barikhol first went to his old distillery, determined to make a fresh batch of Fire Mead.  Unfortunately, the equipment was old and rusted, and within days, a large, highly flammable leak occurred, and soon there was a fire much too large for Barikhol to control!  The Fire Mead was truly afire!

 

Upon Armond 's travels for knowledge he saw a plume of smoke off in the distance.  With his inquisitive nature he had to investigate. As he came to the source of the smoke he saw a small distillery a blaze.  Here was this stout dwarf that Armond meet once before with the Cleric Wyer , frantically trying to put it out. Armond ran to help but it was too late to save anything so Almond persuaded the dwarf to evacuate the distillery.

 

With nothing left to his name, Barikhol allowed himself to be led away by Armond .  They left the hills and travelled towards a small town where they might find food and shelter.  As they made their way through the woods surrounding the small town, they smelled an enticing aroma that was too good to resist.  They followed their nose and came upon a small camp. Armond told Barikhol , we are over there. I will join you in a moment. Barikhol entered the camp and the scene was not as expected. There was a well-built man stirring a pot over a cooking fire.  Lying on the ground next to the fire was a semi-conscious, bloody man.
