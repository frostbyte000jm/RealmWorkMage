Game 31.1 (Armonds Story)

Detailed Entry

As the heroes of Red Larch vanish through the vanishing glass and all that was left was a small pop. Armond turned to the Mud Bloods and said, “I will work on how we are going to get there, get ready. We leave in a 10 day. Hopefully it will not be traveling by glass.”

 

On the day of leaving many showed up. Tyelknowle, Wyer, Otho, Joclyn, Finnis, Grund, and Zummy showed up in gear ready to travel. It was also to most others displeasure that Imusek showed up. Unfortunately to travel the underworld on elemental planes Armond had to hire the help of Imusek to open the ways. Fortunately he wasn’t traveling with them.

 

The heroes traveled to the 4 planes and was able to rescue the souls. Here the souls were formed, but fading. Earth souls were dusting everywhere, Fire souls were slowing smoking away, water souls were turning into a gas, and air souls were leaving floating trails behind them.

 

One the heroes would collect them up they would open a way and it would send everyone back to Faerun. When the spirits would touch land the theory is they would slowly fade and move on to the afterlife of the path they followed.

 

The clerics would help them with this transition.

 

Armond and the rest were meet with plenty of opposition. When they had Fire (the last of the spirits to visit) they went running back though the worlds edge and open the gate. Fighting with tormented spirits they were able to get most through Armond and Ty were the last.

 

When they went to jump a giant swung his tree and knocked Armond to the ground. Ty turned around and started to go back but the portal was almost closed and he couldn’t get through.

 

Leaf helps Armond up as they watch the crowd of spirits coming closer. “Well sir… I guess we were not sure if we were coming home anyway.”

 

“Yeah. Sorry.” Armond tells him.

 

“Don’t be. It has been an honor.” Leaf tells Armond as they start to gather power. “Let’s make this tough for them.”
