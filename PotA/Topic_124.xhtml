Profile

Face of: He was part of the Beleivers, but now owes a Debt to Harburk .

Grund now serves the town as a deputy.

He still sells pickes on 10day.

Grund (male half-orc thug) is the village simpleton. He ekes out a living by making pickles in vats at the end of the field. Children make a game of stealing his pickles. If they do it and he doesn't catch them he lets them have it. The children normally show him that they succeeded. Grund find it fun and enjoys their happiness.
