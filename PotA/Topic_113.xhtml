Story

Ilmeth Waelvur was the owner of Waelvur's Wagonworks. Ilmeth was found to be one of the origional believers by the Heroes. Ilmeth has been sent to Waterdeep for trial.

Stannor Thistlehair, a halfling the Heroes met their first day in town, has taken over the business. Stannor was a drunk because he knew a secret and was scared. The heroes took care of the problem and Stannor got clean.

Stannor lent a horse and a waggon to the heroes on their quest. They never returned. If the heroes replace his wagon, he will pretend like he is getting the bad end of a deal, but reality is the wagon was never his to begin with.
