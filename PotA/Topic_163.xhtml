If you don't know what you're doing and you leave 1 when you return you could end up in your own world, but you could also end up in another world. There are hundreds of possibilities. Many times killing you on entry.

 

Imagine stepping outside your house to find that gravity is 50 times heavier. Or that the air is replaced with something else. It is normally not that drastic, but just as hazardous none the less. A person's body is built for one world it doesn't easily adapt to other worlds. That is unless you know how to travel.

 

Getting a better grip on this knowledge will help Rowena travel. It will help her find what she has been looking for. Rowena is determined and what she needs to learn is here.

It treated the world as a resource and took and took till there was nothing left. After the world was destroyed the evil just left.

 

The world is not regenerating itself. The days are getting hotter and the nights are getting colder. Eventually the world will be inhabitable. Rowena wanted to help, but she knew planes walking these people was more likely to kill them, rather than save them. With a heavy heart Rowena had to leave them. She had limited time. Her enchantments would wear off and the world would treat her as a foreign entity and try to eradicate her.

Downtime Intro 02

 

Not every hero finishes his quest, but hopefully there will always be the one that tries to see it through for him.  

 

Ginny was not your average hand maiden. Ginny was skilled in the arts of deception, stealth, pick pocketing, and spying. Ginny was the hand maiden to Princess Francesca. Princess Francesca knew exactly how skilled Ginny was. Ginny would take care of most espionage and dirty missions that the princess could never be caught doing.

 

These missions were mostly for the good of the land, and then sometimes… it was just for juicy gossip. There were always men coming to Princess Francesca’s village that thought they claimed more right to the leadership then she did. The men never expected to be undermined by a handmaiden. So Ginny would get her hands dirty by spying for info, stealing items that would cause problems, tricking the thugs into thinking one was working against the group, leading them into illegal situations, and more. Ginny was also the one who brought Jax and the princess together.

 

One night the princess was having a stroll by the lake when she saw him. Jax was a paladin knight of a local militia. He had the look that the princess desired. Ginny, never seen the princess really interested in a man before. She knew she liked men, but no one ever caught her eye. Princess Francesca asked Ginny to find out everything she could on Jax. So she went to work. She made one little mistake when he caught her and brought her back to the princess.

 

“I told you I would bring him here.” She tried to quip, but Ginny was actually frustrated that someone finally caught her. This was the start of their beautiful friendship. Within a year Jax and Francesca got married, Ginny was still the hand maiden, but would sometimes, covertly, assist Jax on missions. The princesses knew Ginny wanted more. She was made for adventure, but for some reason Ginny would never leave her side.

 

Everything was picture-perfect until the day that the fire nation attacked. The village didn’t exactly have an military. More like a local force of officers that broke up bar fights or the occasional bad guy. No one was prepared for an invasion. Especially one on this scale. Jax goal was to get to his wife and he fought through the raider’s men and monsters to do so. Ginny was off sleeping in a tree when they came in. She was quick to arms and fought as many as she could. She eventually thought about Francesca and made her way to the keep.

Tom ordered to have the Keep locked down to prevent anyone from getting in. Unfortunately, the forces were too strong. He ran to Francesca to try and protect her, but it was too late as he saw a ball of fire surrounding Francesca. The spell came from a red skinned man standing about 6’2 with thin black hair and some kind of goggles over his eyes. He looks at Tom and smiles. “I hope she wasn’t that important to you.” The flame magician got into her room by blowing a hole in the side of the keep. This was planned, the fire mage knew exactly where to go.

 

Tom, full of rage and hate unleashed everything he had on this red skinned man. This flame magician seemed surprised by Tom’s skills, or it could have been more impressed. Tom did too until he heard the playing of bag pipes. Tom knew it was Odert , the town bard, giving him strength, and that is also when he started noticing the flame magician getting distracted by spells. “Your bard is annoying.” The magician said.

 

Tom replied “Yeah? You should hear him when he hasn’t had anything to drink.”

 

By this time Jax and Ginny ran into the room. Jax processing what just happened to his wife and hoping, just praying, that it may have been someone else. Ginny releases an arrow at the flame magician. He was barely able to deflect the arrow with a burst of fire.

 

“Tell whoever survives the night, that your village had its chance. I made an offer to your wife months ago and she turned me down. I am sorry it had to come to this, but I do not go back on my threats.” The red skinned man said to Jax.

 

“Bastian. The only one that could have saved you tonight you have taken from me. I hope you are at peace with dying.” Jax informed Bastian.

 

“I know what this means, and I am at peace. Good luck my friend.” Bastian said as he unleashed a wall of flames that made everyone jump back. Another arrow shot threw the flames nicking Bastian on the side and making him spin around.

One day Jax stood up from the breakfast table and started suiting up. Ginny was watching him from her tree she adopted that made a nice little resting place. Ginny nodded and whispered to herself. “It is time.” Then Ginny let out a whistle as she backflip out of the tree.

 

Jax put everything together and started heading to the stables. By the time he got there he realized someone was following him. He spun around and it was Ginny, Thomas, and Odert . Jax, a little confused, noticed they were all suited up for traveling. “What are you guys doing?” he asked.

 

Odert replied “My lord, it wasn’t a matter of if you were going to go and avenge the princesses, your wife, it was a matter of when. We have been ready since the moment we arrived, but we were waiting for you to be ready.”

 

Jax, a little teared, said “I guess I can’t talk you out of this?” No one moved, actually Ginny crossed her arms and raised her eyebrow. Jax nodded and said, “Right. Well if we are going to do this you must stop calling me ‘my lord’. I am Jax, I am one of you, and we are in this together.”

 

They traveled north stopping now and then for rest, adventure, food, adventure, entertainment, adventure, and adventure. They found Cult of the Eternal Flame camps and extinguished them every time. They loved everyone they meet, except for people who joined the fire cult. Those people were no longer people and there was nothing anyone could say to change their minds. The cult’s deaths were quick. The only ones they would leave alive are the ones they planned on questioning. After they had answers they would make their deaths quick too.

 

The group traveled to Loudwater in search of a sage. In a recent raid of a Fire Cult's camp they found a lead to the main Fire Cult's camp, and it may even be a lead on how to defeat Bastian Thermandar. They found plans for some kind of device and the person they questioned told them it was a way that allowed the fire cultist to tap into their power. The party couldn't figure it out or knew what it did. This is what led them to Loudwater.

 

They found a girl by the Name of Rowena . Some people in the town described her as very friendly, but a little eccentric. The group tracked her down and showed her what they had.

 

Rowena had never seen something like this before, but she has heard of such a thing. It is rumored that people could create a communications box that will allow connection between two dimensional planes. It would take a very powerful wizard to create such a box. It is also possible that the box is not of this world. Actually, based on the markings and diagrams of what they are showing Rowena and the latest news she has been hearing around the rumor mills. It is very possible this box is from a world with elemental magic. In Rowena 's studies, she has found other objects that have come over to this world from another plane of existence, but most of the time they stop working because they were created with magic of a different world that didn't exist here. If it was elemental magic (or as some people call it DARK MAGIC) it could still work. No one is sure where this elemental magic came from, but it is very rare here and it is new. Elemental Magic also, if other's studies that she read have been right, didn't originate here. After explaining all of this to the group they seemed impressed, but like there were hoping for more.

 

Later that night Ginny found Rowena having a drink on an outside bench of the local tavern. Ginny takes a seat from across from her. Ginny tells Rowena about their adventure so far. She tells her everything. Ginny explains they could use a mage to help them. This is the only lead they have to find Bastian Thermander. Ginny tells Rowena that a wizard took the device to a place called “The stone from nowhere”. Rowena , growing up near here, knew exactly which stone she was talking about. It is called Lance Rock .

 

Lance Rock is made of granite which is what makes it odd. The rock doesn’t match any other stones near it. It is almost like it fell from the sky. There are many legends of this rock as to how it came here, but no one knows the real reason.

 

Rowena knew she was not as seasoned of fighters as Ginny and her crew, but she wanted to help. Rowena opened her mouth to tell Ginny that she will help them and that they needed to go to Lance Rock , but instead pink bubbles came out. Ginny shakes her head a little confused by this and asks, "Are you ok?"

 

Rowena burps and says, "Sorry. Stuff like this happens around me from time to time. Lance Rock is the stone from nowhere. I know where it is."

 

The heroes set off for Lance Rock to find the man who stole the box in hopes this will help lead them to Bastian or at least give them another clue.

 

From this point, this is a story of bad luck. None of which was Rowena 's fault, at least this time. The travel to Lance Rock was rough. The team was pounded on by creatures of all types, terrible weather, and cultist. The party did notice that some of the creatures had flaming red eyes. It was almost as if they were targeting them.

 

They just made it into Lance Rock in the nick of time. An ankheg had chased them all the way into the cave. They barely made it. Tom took the most injuries from acid. The party need to catch its breath, but just then they heard a high pitched, mad sounding, voice.

 

"The lord of lance rock has been waiting for you." Skeletons started rising out of the ground. The group knew it was game over. "The eye knows. The eye always knows. The fire cult sends their regards."

 

They first tried to capture, but the party wasn't going to go down without a fight. It was useless in the end. Jax fell, then Tom, and by this time the party was swarmed.

 

Rowena and Odert were tied up and forced to watch the Necromancer dress up Ginny, Jax, and Tom who were now zombies. The ‘ Lord of Lance Rock ’ forced them to watch their old friends act out skits from childhood plays. The dwarf yelled and scream to just kill him, “end it! I don’t want to see this anymore you fucking bastard.”

 

The necromancer just replied, "Not yet my friend. I have a very special gift. Just for the both of you."

 

Time past, but not too long. Rowena had no idea how many days she has been held captive. There were a couple times where surges went off, but nothing that could help her. The necromancer properly kept her tied and gaged to prevent certain magic.

 

The Lord of Lance Rock finally came in the room with a very frightening looking blade. Odert started to shiver, but Rowena could see the markings. They were none she had ever seen before. "It has been primed. It is ready. You two are blessed." He says talking to his two victims tied up on slabs that were raised up enough so the two could face each other. "Bard, do you have any last words?"

 

The Bard started to speak, but was silenced by a knife going into his chest. At the same time there was a loud noise from the cave. Rowena could swear she heard voices. She tried to scream out, but she was gagged too well. She could make some noise, but not enough that others would hear over the sounds of the rest of the cave.

 

The necromancer said in a whisper to Odert “I was going to say no one would remember it anyway, but someone stole my show.” He then says louder to the room, "What was that? Someone is here? Who would enter my cave? Ok, everyone get ready. Places, places! We will show these intruders not to mess with the Lord of Lance Rock ." He ends with a slight giggle.

 

He runs over to these stairs that has a pull rope. It is an old, almost comically, built trap. He stands there quietly laughing to himself for almost 10 minutes. He finally starts to get impatient. He sends one of his zombies to go out and check.

 

He jumps back down and steps in front of Rowena . "Friends of yours? No matter, I will deal with them just like I dealt with the others." All she can see is her new friend Odert hanging limply on the mostly inverted table. He stabs Rowena with the knife. It wasn't in any place too vital, it could have been worse.

 

She takes [5 damage] to her stomach, but Rowena feels something more, something heavier than just the dagger enter into her skin. The Necromancer stares into her eyes and waits. Rowena sees excitement and relief enter his face. "It worked! IT WORKED!"

 

Seeing the look of confusion on Rowena 's face he says. "Don't worry my dear you will see."
