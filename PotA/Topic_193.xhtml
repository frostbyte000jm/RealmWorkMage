Cleric Temple

Overview

Type: Temple

The temple is still being built, but Wyer has about 20 recruits that he is currently training.

Story

Wyer got favors from the church and built a cleric Temple just outside of Red Larch. Wyer started working for a church of Pelor in Waterdeep. He finally earned enough favor to get this temple built. He has been training for years, this was the best move for him.
