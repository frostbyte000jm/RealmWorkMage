Tyelknowle Backstory

Stories

Clan name - Waa’nornoro [Wha - no - Nor - o]

 

*At age 10, several members of the clan, including Tyelknowle, his mother, father, and brother were captured by slavers.

* At age 18, brother dies in slavery.

* At age 24, Tyelknowle is sold to other slavers.

* At age 26, was being shipped to the Dagon Coast, the slavers got greedy and decided to attack a “Gypsy Caravan” mostly full of Half-elves and Tieflings. The slavers lost and the slaves were freed. Tyelknowle joins the “family”.

* At age 29 moves to Waterdeep to learn more about the “craft” but lives mostly on the street, begging, petty crime, and scrounging.

* At age 35, attempts a “big heist” on Jurras Bigglesworth, a wealthy merchant known to travel frequently. However, Jurras was home. Bigger surprise, Jurras is an influential lay member/supporter of the church of Tymora. Tyelknowle is offered to the chance to work off his debt/crime with the church. Tyelknowle accepts and becomes a member in training.

* At age 41, Tyelknowle makes a pilgrimage out of Waterdeep at the request of the Church (modern day).
