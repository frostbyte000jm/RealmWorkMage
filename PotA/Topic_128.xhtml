Inactive Character List

Overview

Inactive Characters are characters who have not had any screen time lately and the Storyteller doesn’t have any plans on giving them screen time. The storyteller may bring back someone from the inactive list at any time.

 

The players may also bring back anyone from the Inactive Character List and place them on the Active Character List. This tells the storyteller the players are interested to learn more about that character.
