Background

The Allfaiths Shrine is a wayside shrine used by many faiths and owned by none. Priests shuttle out from Waterdeep in pairs for month-long stays. Each pairing includes priests of two or more different faiths arranged by Waterdhavian temples. The visiting priests dwell in simple stone rooms at the back of the temple. They bring their own vestments and holy items and take them away again when their duties end.
