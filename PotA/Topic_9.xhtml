Detailed Entry

The adventure began after the end of the Storm. Armond , Josephus , and Barikhol went out into the town. Barikhol started helping the people of the streets cleanup. Armond went across the street to the tavern and Josephus went down the street to the bakery to help make the Crumble Cakes better. Wyer took a nap.

 

Barikhol talked with Helver and Maegla about the storm and other weird sightings. Maegla called her 4 children out to tell the story.

 

They were out near Lance Rock playing games and picking berries. When all a sudden a Dwarf comes walking up and says to them "get away from here. There be a plague and if you don't want it then you should not come back."

 

The little girl then says she "could hear laughing inside of one of the caves. It was scary." Barikhol said he would check it out.

 

Armond sat down with a bard to find out if he knew of any stories around these parts. He did and he started telling Armond about all these happenings nearby.

 

He told tales about the Haunted Tombs, Bandits, Evil Magic causing bad and unexplainable weather. He talked about a Fire Cultist in Waterdeep that was put to rest. There are old castles to the north west and whatever else Armond wanted to listen to. This Bard was wise and well-traveled. Armond didn't quite know it, but the Bard was excited to help a new adventurer off to the world. He knows that the future holds tough challenges for this hero, but it will be exciting.

 

Wyer was woken up by some commotion down stairs. Kaylessa and Harburk were arguing about needing help. Wyer went down and talked to Kaylessa , and asked her if there is anything he could do.

 

Kaylessa told him about the "fell magic" she has been hearing about in the local area. The storms have been bad and even unexplainable. She believes this is all coming from whatever is happening at Lance Rock .

 

Wyer listen to her story and asked her to keep down the commotion and he and his companions will check out the Rock. She offered payment and maybe free room. 

 

Josephus pours his heart into the Crumble Cake. They make some improvements, but it isn't better yet. It is getting there. After a few more sessions they may be on to something. Mangobarl Lorren thanked Josephus for his time and said he hopes to see him again.

 

Once everyone showed up to the tavern (minus Wyer ) they were greeted by Harburk . Harburk is the town constable. He says that he has been looking for adventurers to deal with this Bandit problem he has been having. He tells them where some Bandits are hiding out near Red Larch .

 

They agreed that Harburk will give them room and board (which apparently means food somehow) for two weeks if they help out the town with these bandits.

 

The heroes the next day head down to where the Bandits are. When they arrived at their camp they could see the Bandits had a bear in a big cage. The adventurers attacked. They quickly plowed through the Bandits. During the fight the Bear broke free and started attacking.

 

Josephus killed the Bear in the end and the party gathered up all the supplies.

 

When they returned to town, they were bloody, tired, and hungry. They went by Haeleeya's bath shop. She said she would start preparing their baths and took them to a room where they could wash up.

 

The Characters went to Swinging Sword to eat. Josephus bumped into the person who has been following them around town. They explained "I noticed your party has been asking a lot of questions around town. As a concerned citizen I know that adventurers normally know more about a town than the locals do. So what I want to know is, what is going on?"

 

Josephus explained that they are just trying to help out around town with some task people need completing. She then offered some advice. "There may be a cave of treasure. Some people came through this town with a map. I remember the location. They were attacked and killed by bandits." she drew a location on a rough map for Josephus . It should be in a cave here. She also gave some landmark descriptions that she saw.

 

Josephus tossed her a silver piece and talked this information over with this group.

 

The next day they decided to head out to the Tomb, the Skull, and the treasure.

 

The Tomb.

 

They found the door to the Tomb ajar slightly. Pushing open the door pots and pans fell crashing to the ground. Entering the tomb they find a table and a rusty old door. Searching around the room they found nothing. They attempt to enter the door they were greeted by a ghost. He asked them to leave.

 

As soon as the heroes obliged a goblin and a half orc came in. They attacked the characters. The characters killed them with only minor injuries.

 

After leaving the tomb the decided to go an check out the Skull.

 

The Skull.

 

Arriving to the skull they saw a weathered human skull, jaw sagging, is pinned to the trunk of a tree ahead by a big black arrow protruding from one of the skull’s eye sockets.

 

Josephus went up to pull the arrow out and once he grabbed it he fell to the ground grabbing his head and screaming. Josephus suffered a hallucination. In his hallucination the skull wakes up and pulls itself off the tree. It chases after him. Josephus reached for his weapons but they were not there. His feet were pinned to the ground his friends had vanished. He was alone. The sky turns an ugly shade of red. Blood rain pours on Josephus . The skull makes his first attack going for his eyes.

 

Once Josephus came to he explained what happened in his hallucination. The heroes decided this was a good place to rest.
