Detailed Entry

"We have no idea how they knew we were here." Teria said to the group. Sesa tightens her grip on her knives.

 

"Look we are just trying to figure out how they knew we were here." Armond reassures.

 

Sesa nods her head towards the guy Armond knocked out. As Grimble is dragging him back to the camp.

 

Grimble threatens their capture by pointing out how Barikhol likes to hit things. Barikhol starts rubbing the prisoner's shoulders and ears to show who is in control.

 

They got the prisoner to give up his name, Hamze, and he also gave up the name of his leader Reash . He mentioned they got word that the Heroes of Red Larch were coming for them so they went to fight them before they got there. Hamze doesn't know how they got word.

 

Rowena came running into the camp screaming "The blue man group knows we're here!"

 

Everyone was surprised to see her. They talked about her disappearance. They even showed her the note she left. Grimble complimented(?) her feather beard.

 

/Cut Scene

Tyelknowle seemed to be in deep thought this whole time. When Ty followed the Mud Bloods back to Waterdeep he was given a proposition from Father Franz . They needed his help. Ty explained "I can't my friends need me. The cults are becoming a big problem and Tymora seems to be leading me down that path."

 

"Could Tymora have brought you to us?" Father Franz rebutted? "Maybe she wanted you to see the problem and then bring you to us where there is a way for you to help combat it."

 

"Tymora will need to show me more evidence before I agree." Ty said

/End Cut Scene

 

While all this was going on Barikhol left and went to go check out the other camp. After following Rowena 's tracks he found it. The camp looked like it had been unoccupied for weeks. Barikhol could find the tracks on the ground of a struggle and saw a body lying face down in the dirt. These were the only evidence that something happened here recently.

 

Barikhol went to flip over the body and jumped out of the way as an electric snake uncoiled at him. The snake slithered off into the night. The body appears to be the man that attacked the camp, but turned invisible.

 

The next day the heroes traveled up to the Larch Path Inn . They stopped here for food and take a short break before continuing their journey down the side trail that leads towards the vale of dancing waters . While they were there they meet with two guards from Waterdeep. They showed them the water cult tattoo on the prisoner and turned him over to them. One guard was eying Ty closely.

 

The heroes went to venture up the path and only made it about 5 minutes up the road when a group of guards on horseback caught up. It was the guards from the Larch Path Inn . "Sir." They say to Tyelknowle "We have some questions for you and we would like you to come with us."

 

Ty askes "What is this about?"

 

The guard approaches him. "we have an eye witness that says you stabbed and killed a fella outside the Larch Path inn ." He holds up a picture of the victim. It was Expo . He holds up another drawing and it kind of looks like Ty. "I think you need to come with us."

 

"No! This is Bullshit. Expo was part of a band of highwaymen. We were defending ourselves." Ty proclaimed.

 

"The witness said the man was tied up." one of the guards said.

 

Ty readied himself to fight the guards if needed, but he looked over at the other guard that spoke up and saw him wink. He looked back at the lead guard and noticed his weapon was an Axe and he was wearing an amber ring. Ty glanced down at his hand and closed it. "Tymora help me." he said as he relaxed and agreed to go with the guards.

 

"Let me get this cleared up and I will meet you back in Red Larch ." Ty said to the others.

 

/Cut Scene

We get a flashback of Ty and Joclyn in some hallway light by torch light. Ty leaning up against the wall and Joclyn standing next to him. We catch them in the middle of a conversation. We can tell it has been going on for a while.

 

"You're the only one who can do this." Joclyn said to Ty. "We need you. None of us have the skills to help this man. You do. And… you kind of owe me."

/End cut

 

The heroes, worried if they will ever see Ty again, continued up the Larch Path.

 

In the shadow of the night, the heroes are monitoring the keep that sits on the water, south east of the vale. They see movement in the keep due to torch light and they can make out a guard on every wall. Seeing through the slits where the front gate is located Barikhol counts 3 - 4 guards in the tower.

 

Grimble feels there must be another way in. The Keep is old and under repair. It is possible there is a secret path or something to get on the inside instead of walking up to the gate.

 

Sesa and Barikhol took off full sprint towards the castle. They were very quiet in their movements. Sesa looks over and down at the dwarf. She glances at his boots and back up at him. "You're extremely fast…" leaving off the condescending "For a dwarf". She was impressed and didn't want it to come off as rude. Barikhol smiled and lifted his eyebrows to Sesa .

 

After some searching they found an arrow slit on the north east side of the castle was wide enough to let people squeeze through. Part of the wall was missing and it was only 8 feet up.

 

After informing the group, they made their way over to the arrow slit. They had to move extra slowly due to a couple in chainmail. When they arrived they could hear snoring in the room.

 

Barikhol was first to jump up. He took a running start and kicked off a few notches in the wall. He grabbed onto the ledge and the handhold came loose. He hit the ground with a thud and everyone held their breath.

 

The snoring paused and started up again, but was slightly louder.

 

Barikhol tried again and pulled himself up. He reached out the window to help pull Rowena up, but as she was being lifted Barikhol was attacked.

 

The fight started with one Bugbear hitting Barikhol from behind, and the other two starting to rise from their beds.

 

Grimble pulls himself quickly up there and after a quick prayer and throwing his arm in front of him, a red light glows around the 3 bugbears. Teria jumped up there, but couldn't get by and reached down to help Rowena and Sesa up. Sesa tried to also push herself through, but couldn't.

 

Rowena started humming and the two bugbears shook their heads, but the 3rd bugbear who was still on the bed, fell down to sleep.

 

A purple and blue light shoot in from the arrow slit to the north and Armond landed in the room in the room sliding. He had his hands out in front of him and pushed a purple and black light towards the back of a bugbear. It hit him and the bugbear hit the wall.

 

A morning star was lifted above Barikhol 's head and Grimble blocked it as Barikhol kicked one in the knee and hit the other with flat of his hand. He spun around kicking the first with his heal making him stumble backwards while dropping to his right hand and double kicking the second one moving him back. Landing with his feet behind him in a squat he rolled to bring both fist up lunging forward and pushing them back far enough for the ladies to get in the room. Barikhol then runs up knees off the second bugbear and goes to slam his fist down on the left one. The Bugbear swings his morning star around Barikhol 's wrist and slam him to the ground. The bugbear pulls back on the morning star and drives it down onto the monk.

 

Grimble then goes and swings his hammer as the bugbear is concentrating on Barikhol . He gets hit from the side then slams the warhammer down, but the bugbear moves out of the way.

 

Teria goes running up to the Bugbear on the right and sliding down to her knees while the morning star swings over her head she drives her rapier into the bugbears leg. Rowena starts to conjure a ball of flame to throw at the bugbear. Sesa seeing what she is doing runs and leaps using Teria to land on the other side of the bugbear, she drives her knives into his back on the way down.

 

Once Sesa lands she grabs the bugbear by the hair and pulls his head back as Rowena throws the fire bolt. It enters under the bugbears chin and out the top of his head.

 

The sounds of magic pulling conjuring something physical could be heard through the room as Armond pulls the force into his hand crafting a glave. He runs up and pokes the bugbear to move him into position for Barkhol to take out.

 

Barikhol kips up, swings his leg into the bugbears kneecap, and starts hyper punching the bugbear in the stomach. The bugbear starts to collapse and Barikhol drives his elbow into his throat.

 

The morning star starts to fall to the stone floor and Barikhol catches it before it hits.

 

We see Barikhols face and the room has a faint red glow. Barikhols breathing gets heavier when we see blood splash up onto the wall, on his face, on the bed, the floor, and we hear a thud over, and over, and over…

 

Grimble grabs Barikhols arm and Barikhol looks over him violently. "It done. He is dead." Barikhol realizes what he is doing and drops the morning star.

 

They look around the room and Rowena is missing.

 

Rowena is standing in the room when the surroundings go fuzzy. It is almost like Rowena has tunnel vision. She looks around and can see everyone, but her prevail vison has gone fuzzy.

 

"Where did Rowena go?" Armond asks.

 

"I'm right here!" Rowena says, but no one reacted. It is almost like they can't hear or see her.

 

Rowena looks over and sees her fox spirit. It has one of those doggy grins on his face. Trying to yell again "I'M RIGHT HERE!!!!" Rowena reappears and almost scared Armond , who was standing right next to the window.

 

"Son of a bitch!" Armond says

 

"Oh," Rowena realizes, "You can see me now."

 

Sesa scouts for the group and they go across the path to the chapel. There is a single female inside praying.

 

The heroes sneak in and surround her. She almost screams and Barikhol quickly quiets her with a threat. She questions who they are and they lie and say they are new recruits of Reash . She thinks "Of course Reash 's thugs are roaming the keep."

 

They question what is going on and other things. The lady is upset that her master has died. " Drosnin was killed by the Heroes of Red Larch ." she says with all the disdain she can muster.

 

Most of the questions they ask she tells them to ask their master or not to concern themselves with such things. She finally goes back to her prayers and then Barikhol puts her in a sleeper hold, ties her up, and hides her under the table.

 

Armond sees a knife on the table that has some text he is not familiar with on it, and takes it.

 

The heroes peek out the door and see there is a group of people walking down one of the interior paths. They pull their heads back in and decide what to do next.
