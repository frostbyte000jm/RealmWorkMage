Game Day

Game Date: 7/31/16

Date In Play:: (5) Mirtul 08, 1491;

TLDR

Detailed Entry

The heroes making their trek to the Feathergale Spire see the large tower off in the distance. Above the tower flies hippogriffs and giant vultures with guards flying on them.

 

Josephus talks to the party. "I feel we may have missed something with the earth cult back at Red Larch . I mean Larrakh  just disappeared without a trace. Someone has to know something. I think we should try and find him and close this case."

 

Armond and Barikhol thought it would be a good idea to at least check out the spire while they all were here. Armond doesn't feel there is more to the case until new evidence is found.

 

Larrakh left town and the Beleivers were caught, questioned, and sent to Waterdeep for trial. Sure there may have been some people who may have been a part of the conspiracy that were not caught, but what are the heroes going to do? Round up everyone in town and interrogate them?

 

The heroes walked up to the drawbridge and pulled the rope to ring the bell. The Spire is built on a rock in the middle of a cavern. The natural built moat is about 20 feet from the edge to the spire and several hundred feet down.

 

"Good afternoon fine travelers! What brings you to Feathergale Spire ?" A female said looking out one of the windows.

 

The heroes introduced themselves and where they hail from.

 

The woman answered back "The same adventurers that brought down the earth cult conspiracy in Red Larch ?"

 

The party looked at each other a little cautious that, a person/group they don't know, knows them. Armond finally answers "yes, the same."

 

"Well, why didn't you say so?" the woman replies, "Any friend of Red Larch is a friend of ours!"

 

The drawbridge lowers and the woman comes walking across the bridge with two armed guards. The guards stop a safe distance to show they are not a threat and the lady moves forward. Barikhol is the first to greet her. He extends his arm and grasps hers. "Ello me lady," Barikhol begins and moves his arm around her to bring her forward and introduce her to his friends. "I am Barikhol I am pleased to meet you. These are my companions and friends, Armond , Josephus , Ty, and Rowena ."

 

The lady seemed a little surprised by Barikhol 's friendliness and second introductions, but decided to smile to not seem rude. "My name is Savra Belabrantha. I am a Feathergale Knight and we would be pleased to accept you as guest."

 

[Cut Scene for Armond ]

Armond was contacted by his host. “Something is wrong with her” it told him.

[End Cut Scene]

 

On entry into the Front hall Savera stops and request the heroes to store their weapons. After she took them up to see the Lord Commander. They passed by the Kitchen, Solarium, the Great Hall, the Knights Rooms, and finally they made it up to the Pinnacle.

 

It was a very long climb and the tower seems bigger than it appears. Once they get to the Pinnacle they were looking out at the rest of the Sumber Hills and noticing they were hundreds of feet above the hills.

 

The Lord Commander stood on the edge of the north point watching his knights fly on Vultures and Hippogriffs. He turns around and sees Savera bringing in the party and goes to greet them.

 

"Hail! I am Thurl Merosska, Lord Commander of the Feathergale Knights . Welcome to the Feathergale Spire . Who are our honored guest?" the Lord Commander Merosska asks.

 

Savera begins introductions allowing the Lord Commander to greet them all. Then interjects that they are the adventurers from Red Larch who drove out the Earth Cult. Thurl looks impressed and ask them "Really? That was you?"

 

Armond inquires "How is it that you know about us?"

 

Thurl responds, "The Feathergale society are knights of Waterdeep. Don't forget that Red Larch reports to Waterdeep. They are neighbors of ours and I make it a point to read their reports."

 

The Heroes had some questions about the Knights. One of the questions was "is it possible that your knighthood has been infiltrated by one of these cults?"

 

The Lord Commander considers this for a moment, then brushes this off as not possible. "Our men are well vetted. The idea of this seems very unlikely." The Lord Commander asks a few questions of his own.

"Who do you serve your allegiance to?"

 

The heroes admitted they serve allegiance only to themselves, but Josephus and Armond have served in the military before, but are now retired to do adventuring on their own.

 

When The Lord Commander question why they are doing this? The heroes explained there was monetary compensation involved and even though they do want to help, they also need to make a living.

 

"This question is the most important… … Are you hungry?"

 

Everyone was very hungry.

 

Thurl says to Savera "Please, take our guest and get them rooms. Give them what they need so they may clean up and rest up. Feast will be served in a couple hours."

 

The heroes got cleaned up and ready for feast. Josephus helped out in the kitchen. Barikhol noticed when being ushered to their seats they were being placed in a position that would favor the knights if they chose to attack. He got the insight that they are being cautious of their visitors rather than being malicious.

 

During feast Thurl told a tale:

"I remember my first adventure. My troop was sent to the village of Frown to investigate missing children. When we arrived the entire town clammed up. They acted like nothing happened. You don't notice something till it is gone. Imagine walking around a village full of people, but no children. Not a single one. It was eerie. It wasn't for a couple days trying to get some information till a mother came up to me and told me "They were playing out in the fields". Her husband took her away.


I went to the fields to investigate. I tried to find tracks, toys, anything that would point me to the children. I remember most of the troop was out there with me, searching. I knocked over a scarecrow, and when I was sitting it back up trying to get it in place, I saw the eyes move. They looked right at me. They were human eyes. The kids were turned into scarecrows.
 

We traced it down to a witch that lived in the town. After killing her we brought in a Cleric to undo the enchantment.
Frown still celebrates this day as the day the children returned."

 

Thurl says to the Heroes "Honored guest, please tell us about your adventures. We want to hear your tales."

 

Armond stood up and was clearly not ready for this. He tried to tell them about the adventure of the necromancer, but kept getting details mixed up and having to start over (he got a 3 on his performance). The Knights were trying to help improve the story and help him embellish parts, but it wasn't working to well. At the end they all sat there in silence not sure if it was over and then all yell out "Huzzah!"

 

//cut scene//

Thurl leans over an whispers to one of his knights, "They really need a bard."

 

Rowena could be spotted with her palm in her face shaking her head.

//End Cut//

 

Just then a guard comes running in and yells out, "A Manticore has been spotted in the Valley!" The knights start getting excited and Thurl says "I'll tell you what. The person who brings me the head of the Manticore will receive my ring." The ring on his finger is a really nice golden ring.

 

Everyone starts getting up and heading to their post. Savera sees the heroes talking among themselves and informs them "we have extra mounts if they want to join in on the hunt." They agree, grab their gear, and head up to the Pinnacle.

 

Tyelknowle and Rowena approach the rest of the heroes and tell them "We are going to stay behind. It may be opportunistic that the spire will be unmanned. We may be able to have a look around and see if there is anything going on."

 

Armond agrees and asks Ty "Could you detect magic on Savera for me?" Ty agrees, but inquires as to why, "It is just a hunch."

 

On the Pinnacle there are knights getting to their mounts and getting ready to head out. The stable master is up there helping. When the heroes come up the hippogriffs turn to the heroes and stare them down. ( "ok bow" ) The stable master introduces the heroes to the hippogriffs and as soon as everyone is introduced the creatures relax.

 

The hunt begins. The heroes flew out over the valley. It was after sundown but the sky was still lit. They didn't have long till darkness took over and finding the Manticore would be hopeless.

 

After some searching the heroes found the beast. Barikhol blew the horn to signal the rest of the knights and they started chasing. The heroes had a little difficulty as they were not used to flying mounts. They had a few close calls, but there was one time that Armond fell off. Lucky for magic, he was saved.

 

Barikhol and Josephus caught up to the Manticore and started fighting. The ground must have been very muddy. Attacks were missing and everyone, including the Manticore, was having trouble landing a blow. Finally Armond got his creature to start trusting him again and they flew over to the fight.

 

The Knights started riding in to help the heroes, but as soon as they got there they were attacked by vultures. Vultures were falling left and right on the battle field. One of the knights fell from his mount and landed hard on the ground. Josephus barely got out of the way.

 

After many attacks and many critical errors, on both sides, the heroes finally killed the Manticore.

 

The knights were able to take care of their swarm of vultures and two road down to see if the heroes needed any help. After making sure everyone was ok they started to ride off. One of the knights took a look at Armonds Hippogriff and noticed it was all muddy, had twigs, and parts of bushes hanging off of it. It also had really wide eyes. The knights chuckled to themselves knowing that they all have crashed before. Everyone flew off into the night back to the Spire.

 

//Cut Scene//

A male walks up to a female out in the valley and says to her "It appears the Manticore was no real match of them. They may be stronger than we thought."

 

The female replies without looking at the man, "it is ok. I may have better use of them alive." She reaches down and picks up a bird cage that has a silhouette of a pixie inside. The pixie has a nervous shake to it.

//End Cut//

 

//Ty and Rowena Cut//

Ty and Rowena waited till everyone was out of the castle and made their way through the halls. After looking in a few rooms Rowena saw Savera and nudges Ty.

 

Ty tells Rowena "Go talk to Savera about the tapestries in the Great Hall. I need her distracted for about 10 minutes." Rowena gives Ty a questioning look, but decides he must have a plan.

 

Rowena goes up to Savera and tells her, "I get nervous when I can't be out there with my team."

 

Savera replies, "I know exactly what you mean. They will be fine. The Knights will keep an eye on them."

 

Rowena looks up at the tapestries and how they have depictions of battles on hippogriffs. "So, can you tell me about these?"

 

Savera gives a questioning look to Rowena and Rowena notices Savera look at her ears. Savera knows she was caught and gives a quick "sorry, I was just curious." she then turns back to the tapestries and begins, "This first one here was a battle that took place over 100 years ago. This was the first time the hippogriffs went flying into battle. This was obviously before the current Lord Commander.

 

Waterdeep was under attack by ghost. Waterdeep has griffon riders, but they are 5 riders. Waterdeep knew the ghost were coming but didn't have the man power to hold them back. The ghost were going to make an aerial attack and a group of heroes knew how to defeat them, but they needed time. A group of knights were sent to the Sumber Hills to use the hippogriffs. Waterdeep had a stable out there were they were training hippogriffs, but they have never been ridden into any kind of battle. At least not by the knights of Waterdeep.

 

The heroes ventured to where the sorcerer was summoning the ghost from and had to fight his undead army to destroy the gym. The Griffon riders of Waterdeep were losing. There were hundreds of ghost and the Griffon Riders are mighty, but even they didn't stand a chance at survival for long.

 

Then came the sounds of the horns of the Hippogriff riders. This turned the ties of the battle. The knights fought together and were able to hold the ghost back long enough for the adventures on the ground to do their job."

 

"Savera!" The Lord Commander called coming into the Great Hall. "I need you to come with me to the Pinnacle. The knights are being attacked by vultures." The Lord Commander sees that she was in conversation with Rowena and he paused "If you don't mind," speaking to Rowena , "I need to borrow my knight."

 

Rowena nodded and wished them luck. Rowena glanced over at Ty and he gave her the thumbs up.

 

They kept searching the rest of the Spire when they made it to Thurl Merosska's room. Inside they found a letter. They were about to take it, but thought that he may notice it. So they copied it instead. If they wanted to build a case against him taking the letter would be the better option, but Ty wasn't sure they had enough with just this.

//End Ty and Rowena Cut//

 

The Heroes came back and everyone celebrated as Barikhol , Josephus , and Armond were carrying the head of the Manticore. As promised the Lord Commander makes a show of it by taking off his ring in the Great Hall and gives it to the heroes. The celebration continues.

 

The Ty and Rowena approach the rest of the party. Rowena tells the group "We think we may have found some something."

 

At the same time the knight that fell on the battle field was being carried down stairs. They call out for help. Ty starts to make his way after them, but pauses as he makes eye contact with Armond . He tells Armond , "You were right. The magic seems to be concentrated around her brain." He paused for Armond to make a response.

 

Armond "Really? Did you find out any more?"

 

Ty, wanting to tell him more, "Yeah, but not right now, I got to go." Armond nods with understanding.

 

Rowena hands the group a note. The note read:

" Merosska,

 

We are pleased to hear about the outcome of your altercation with the Black Earth cult, and we praise you for the capture of one of their prisoners. This noblewoman from Waterdeep has an interesting tale to tell, and we shall enjoy interrogating her further. Keep a close watch on the Sacred Stone Monastery . I want to know what our enemy is planning next.

 

Your beloved queen,

Aerisi Kalinoth"
