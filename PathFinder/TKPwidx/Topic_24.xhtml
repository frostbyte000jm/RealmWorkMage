Orcs

Overview

Orcs resemble some sort of feral human but with much more bestial features, most prominently they have oversized, boar-like canines and sharp teeth. Orcs are normally around six feet in height; however, they often seem much larger due to their intimidating muscles. Orcs scar easily so even the youngest orc warrior will already bear an intimidating array of scars. Orc skin tone varies between a sallow pink through various shades of grey to dark green. Orcs possess small, beady eyes that are very sensitive to sunlight; their facial features are vaguely pig-like; and their hair is wiry like a boar's bristles. On average, an orc weighs around 210 pounds.

Story

When Orcs eyes turn red, this means they are raging and on their last limb.

Orcs do not, usually, think in the long term for tactics of fights.
