Game Notes

Day Three - Date: unknown (still); Location: woods outside of White Ridge

 

We got a map and it has a big camp, the lumber camp, and three smaller camps. We wanted to scout a little so we spotted the main camp. They have four prisoner’s, 3 orcs , 1 badazz leader, 2 Worgs.

We bravely ran away.
We rested and the next day informed the Foreman of the map and the prisoners. He asked us to help.

It was decided that we would inspect one of the smaller camps.

We got there and Autumn noticed that two of the orcs had also been at the main site. We waited for a few hours and once one of them left we attacked the others.
the other one came back later, dug out his horn from the stone around the fire and blew. We killed him.
We then moved away to plan some more.

 

A further plan was discussed. Master Alchemist Luca and myself were badly wounded. The horn called 3 more orcs and we bravely ran away… again.

 

I’m not sure what we did to offend her, but the Kami Princess Autumn must have taken our failure hard and she punished me and the Master Alchemist. Fortunately, Master Alchemist Luca had an extra potion.

The next morning, the heroes formed a plan. Kami Princess Autumn would set off to the southeast and cause a distraction. The other heroes would come in from the north west. Once she blew the horn, she would use her mighty magic and join us in the main camp.

Amazingly, the Kami was able to sneak into the camp in the belly of the Warg ! Once we arrived, she popped out of the Warg and inspired us to attack the orcs ! Thankfully, Pepsi the Sprite also appeared along with Glade. I did what I could to keep the leader orc occupied, while I was able to strike him a couple times, he struck me down. Fortunately Master Alchemist Luca was there to save me. Front-man Vyx never stopped inspiring us to even greater deeds. Master Alchemist Luca ’s blazing concoctions flew through the field even while Pepsi’s sparks rained down upon our enemies. Master Vyn was all over the field, drawing the ire of the enemy. At one point the Warg grasped him in it’s jaws, but Master Vyn was not to be confined as before.

In all, it was amazing to see and the prisoners had nothing to worry about. I’m so glad they were able to save them.
