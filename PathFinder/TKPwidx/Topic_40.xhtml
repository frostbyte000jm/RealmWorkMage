Game Notes

I am taking the time to write a little in my journal while we rest and clean up. After writing in my journal this morning, going through my morning exercises, and breaking my evening fast, I attempted to learn my location in the world. I learned the name of the county and the nearest, largest city, called Starfall.

 

Regardless, there is not much I can do currently. I believe I am north in whatever country or continent I am on. Eventually I will travel further north if I can reach the Crown of the World and perhaps reach my home. I believe the Kami, Princess Autumn , also lives to the north. Perhaps we can help her. Although I must confess, I’m at a loss as to what help she needs. I believe she is asking for a place for her people to live? but seeing as they are spirits, is it not right for them to return to their place of origin? Is she asking for people to plant gardens or restore a forest? I am sad that her needs are beyond my understanding.  

 

Master Vyn has decided he will investigate a symbol he found to see if there is something deeper into these attacks on the local area. He is indeed wise, I would never have thought to follow up on such things. I expect that some people are just driven by their natural desires. He came to us while I was listening to Front-man Vzx (I am unsure of the title). She was very entertaining although the details of her story are a bit embellished. I enjoy listening and watching her. It does make me miss some of the more relaxing flute, erhu, and huqin.

 

Master Luca has agreed to go with us. It is satisfying to have such stalwart companions. I believe I will do what little I can to help. I need to earn a lot more money to get me, the Ox, and the Dog back home with our package.

 

Thus I find myself in the middle of the woods. We spoke to a beleaguered lumber foreman. They have had a couple attacks and lost an entire work crew over the last week. He remembered seeing a similar talisman to the one Master Vyn found. Silly me, I forgot my shirt and pack.

 

The Kami was excellent about guiding us in the woods as one would expect. We came to place the work crew that disappeared last seen. Fortunately, we were able to determine a direction even after such a long time. We went in that general direction and discovered a sort of scout camp with more orcs and a terrible wolf creature that Vyx told us (later) was a Warg . Again, I showed my ignorance and stepped out into the open in order to question these vicious people. I don’t think they could even understand me. Fortunately (and amazingly) Vyx called upon a light spirit to protect me. The others rushed into my rescue as well. The Kami’s owl, Glade (I believe) was also amazing.

 

I was terrified and full of dread when I saw the wolf creature swallow Master Vyn ! He was trying to heal me and keep me safe and this is what I cost him. I grabbed the creature and the others bombarded  with spells and components and Master Vyn was able to crawl out what must have been a gross and truly frightening experience. I am amazed that he kept a presence of mind to be able to call forth his ancestors to aid him even while swallowed.

 

I hope it was worth the distress; we did find a sort of map. The markings lead us to believe that there are three such camps as this one. It also noted the location of the Logging camp as well as another location that we suspect the lumberjacks (if they are alive) are being kept.

 

We discussed our next actions. Master Vyn showed much wisdom in wanting to return to the village to share our knowledge and rest. I admit, despite it not being noon yet, it has been a full day. Front-man Vyx suggested we at least see the last camp to learn what we can of the enemy and the state of the prisoners. I am inclined to agree with her since time may be important in a rescue attempt. If things are well, we can leave and get help. I will of course bow to the wisdom of this noble party. Perhaps their strength inspires me to deeds beyond my ability.

 

we shall see.

 

When surrounded in darkness, it is important to be able to create one’s own light.
